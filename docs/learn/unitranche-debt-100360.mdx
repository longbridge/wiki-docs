# Unitranche Debt

<ArticleMeta id={100360} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Unitranche debt is a loan structure that combines the characteristics of both senior and subordinated debt into a single loan facility. Unlike traditional multi-tiered financing structures, unitranche debt consolidates different layers of debt into one, simplifying the financing process for the borrower and reducing legal and administrative costs. Unitranche debt is often used in mergers, acquisitions, leveraged buyouts, and other complex financing transactions. While it typically comes with a higher interest rate, it offers greater flexibility and speed.</p>
