# Days Working Capital

<ArticleMeta id={102617} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:02'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Days working capital describes how many days it takes for a company to convert its working capital into revenue.&nbsp;</p><p>The more days a company has of working capital, the more time it takes to convert that working capital into sales. The higher the days working capital number the less efficient a company is.</p>
