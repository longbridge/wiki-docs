# Long-Term Loans

<ArticleMeta id={278} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:57:17'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Long-term loans refer to the funds borrowed by a company from banks or other financial institutions with a maturity period of more than one year. These loans are typically used for long-term investments and operations, such as acquiring fixed assets or expanding business activities. Long-term loans are listed as liabilities on the company's balance sheet, reflecting its long-term financing structure and debt repayment capacity.</p>
