# Long-Term Assets

<ArticleMeta id={101181} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Long-term assets are&nbsp;assets, whether tangible or non-tangible, that will benefit the company for more that one year. Also known as&nbsp;non-current assets, long-term assets can include&nbsp;fixed assets&nbsp;such as a company's property, plant, and equipment, but can also include other assets such as long term investments,&nbsp;patents, copyright, franchises, goodwill, trademarks, and trade names, as well as software. Long-term assets can be contrasted with current assets, which can be conveniently sold, consumed, used, or exhausted through standard business operations with one year.</p>
