# Short-term Loans

<ArticleMeta id={197} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:01:53'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Short-term loans refer to loans that a company will repay within one year or within one operating cycle exceeding one year. Short-term loans are usually used to solve the problem of insufficient short-term funds turnover in the company, and have relatively short repayment periods and higher interest rates. The ability of a company to manage its short-term borrowings is a crucial indicator of its liquidity and financial health.</p><p><br/>&nbsp;</p>
