# Non-Purpose Loan

<ArticleMeta id={101381} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A non-purpose loan is a type of loan where the borrowed funds are not restricted to a specific use. Borrowers can use the loan proceeds for any legitimate purpose such as personal consumption, investments, paying off debts, and more. Unlike purpose-specific loans (e.g., mortgage loans, auto loans), non-purpose loans offer greater flexibility in how the funds can be used. However, they typically come with higher interest rates as the lender cannot control the specific use of the funds, thereby facing higher risk.</p>
