# Trading Financial Liabilities

<ArticleMeta id={13} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:10:29'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Trading financial liabilities refer to financial liabilities issued by enterprises for short-term investment or trading, usually measured at market prices and can be bought or sold in the market.</p>

Case 2: A financial institution uses trading financial liabilities for market arbitrage operations, profiting from price differences between different markets.
