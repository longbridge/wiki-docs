# Leverage Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={100457} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Leverage Ratio is a financial metric used to measure the degree to which a company is utilizing debt to finance its operations. It reflects the proportion of debt in a company's capital structure, thereby assessing the financial risk and debt-servicing capability of the company. The level of leverage ratio can impact a company's credit rating, financing costs, and financial stability.</p>
