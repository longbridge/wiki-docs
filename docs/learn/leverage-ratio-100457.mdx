# Leverage Ratio

<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

A leverage ratio is any one of several financial measurements that look at how much capital comes in the form of debt (loans) or assesses the ability of a company to meet its financial obligations.
