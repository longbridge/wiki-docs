# Growth Factor

<ArticleMeta id={325} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:10'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Growth factor refers to financial metrics and data used to assess a company's growth potential. Common growth factors include revenue growth rate, earnings growth rate, and earnings per share (EPS) growth rate. Investors analyze these growth factors to evaluate a company's future growth prospects and make investment decisions accordingly. Growth factors play a crucial role in growth-oriented investment strategies.</p>
