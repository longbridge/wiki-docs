# Market Order

<ArticleMeta id={100164} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Market Order is a type of trading instruction that directs a broker or trading platform to buy or sell a security immediately at the current market price. The primary characteristic of a market order is its speed of execution, as it does not set a specific price limit and will be executed as long as there is liquidity. Market orders are suitable for investors who want to quickly enter or exit the market but carry the risk of execution at a less favorable price during market volatility.</p>
