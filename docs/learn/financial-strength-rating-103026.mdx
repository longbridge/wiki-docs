# Financial Strength Rating

<ArticleMeta id={103026} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:17:51'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Financial strength rating is an assessment of the financial condition of a financial institution or company, typically conducted by rating agencies. It aims to help investors, creditors, and other stakeholders understand the financial health and credit risk of the entity being assessed. The rating is usually expressed in letter grades, with AAA being the highest grade, indicating a very strong financial condition, while grades C or D indicate a weaker financial condition.</p>
