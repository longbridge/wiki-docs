# Current Assets

<ArticleMeta id={183} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:02:45'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Current assets refer to assets that can be quickly converted into cash or cash equivalents in the course of business operations, typically including cash, accounts receivable, prepaid accounts, inventory, short-term investments, etc. Current assets are an important component of business operations and can be used to support daily operations and repay debts.
