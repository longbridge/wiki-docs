# Fair Value

<ArticleMeta id={100476} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Fair value is the price that would be received to sell an asset or paid to transfer a liability in an orderly transaction between market participants at the measurement date. It reflects the true market value of an asset or liability under current market conditions, rather than its book value or historical cost. Determining fair value typically involves considering market prices, the behavior of market participants, and current market conditions.</p>
