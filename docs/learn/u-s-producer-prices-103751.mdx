# U.S. Producer Price Index

<ArticleMeta id={103751} updatedAt={'2023-10-12 02:22:21'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The U.S. Producer Price Index (PPI) is an economic indicator published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics that measures the average price changes received by producers when selling their products. It is an index that includes various goods and services, reflecting price changes in the production process.</p>
