# Yield Variance

<ArticleMeta id={100292} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Yield Variance refers to the difference between the actual output and the expected or standard output, commonly used to measure the efficiency and effectiveness of a production process. In industries like manufacturing or agriculture, yield variance can indicate how effectively resources are being utilized during production. Specifically, yield variance can be caused by various factors such as the quality of raw materials, the performance of production equipment, the skill level of workers, and losses during the production process. By analyzing yield variance, companies can identify issues within the production process and take corrective actions to improve efficiency and reduce costs.</p>
