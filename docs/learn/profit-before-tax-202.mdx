# Profit Before Tax

<ArticleMeta id={202} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:01:41'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Profit Before Tax (PBT) is the net profit a company earns after subtracting all operating costs, administrative expenses, selling expenses, depreciation, and interest expenses, but before deducting income tax. This metric reflects the company's profitability after accounting for all operating expenditures and provides investors and management with a crucial measure of the company's operational performance.</p><p>Profit&nbsp;Before&nbsp;Tax=Total&nbsp;Revenue−Operating&nbsp;Costs−Administrative&nbsp;Expenses−Selling&nbsp;Expenses−Depreciation−Interest&nbsp;Expenses</p>
