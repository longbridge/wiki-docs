# Upside/Downside Tasuki Gap

<ArticleMeta id={102766} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:02'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Upside/Downside Tasuki Gap is a candlestick pattern in technical analysis that indicates a price gap in an uptrend or downtrend, followed by two consecutive white candlestick lines. An upside Tasuki gap suggests that the price may continue to rise, while a downside Tasuki gap indicates that the price may continue to fall.</p>
