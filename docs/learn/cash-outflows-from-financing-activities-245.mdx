# Cash Outflows from Financing Activities

<ArticleMeta id={245} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:59:40'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Cash outflow from financing activities refers to the amount of cash paid out by a company through financing activities. Financing activities refer to the activities by which a company raises funds through issuing stocks or debts. Cash outflow from financing activities mainly includes cash used for debt repayment, cash used for dividend payment, cash used for interest payment, etc.
