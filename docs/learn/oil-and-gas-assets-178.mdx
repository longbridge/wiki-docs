# Oil and Gas Assets

<ArticleMeta id={178} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:02:57'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Oil and gas assets refer to assets owned by a company that are related to oil and gas exploration, development, production, etc. Oil and gas assets typically include oil and gas wells, extraction equipment, pipelines, storage facilities, etc. For energy companies, oil and gas assets are one of their main assets and have a significant impact on their operating performance and value.
