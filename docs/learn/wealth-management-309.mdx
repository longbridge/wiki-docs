# Wealth Management

<ArticleMeta id={309} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:40'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Wealth management refers to the process of managing and increasing the wealth of individuals and institutions through a series of comprehensive services, such as asset allocation, investment management, and risk management. Wealth management aims to meet clients' financial goals and provide personalized investment advice and solutions. Wealth management typically includes asset allocation, investment portfolio management, tax planning, retirement planning, insurance planning, etc.
