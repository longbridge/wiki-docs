# Reserve Fund

<ArticleMeta id={100065} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A reserve fund is a savings account or other highly liquid asset set aside by an individual or business to meet&nbsp;any future costs or financial obligations, especially those arising unexpectedly. If the fund is set up to meet the costs of scheduled upgrades, less liquid assets may be used.&nbsp;</p><p>For example, a homeowner's&nbsp;association&nbsp;often manages a reserve fund to help maintain the community&nbsp;and its amenities using the dues paid by homeowners.</p>
