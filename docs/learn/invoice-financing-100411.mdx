# Invoice Financing

<ArticleMeta id={100411} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Invoice Financing is a type of business financing where a company uses its outstanding invoices (i.e., invoices that customers have not yet paid) as collateral to obtain a loan or advance from a financial institution or financing company. There are two main forms of invoice financing: Invoice Discounting and Invoice Factoring.</p>
