# Cash Inflows from Operating Activities

<ArticleMeta id={209} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:01:13'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Operating cash inflow refers to the total amount of cash inflow generated by a company in its operating activities. This includes cash received from sales of goods, provision of services, tax refunds received, and other cash received related to operating activities.These cash inflows reflect the company's ability to generate cash from its core business activities and are crucial for assessing the company's financial health and operational efficiency.</p>
