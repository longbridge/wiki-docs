# Net Increase in Pledged Loans

<p>The net increase in pledged loans refers to the net increase in pledged loans within a certain period. Net increase refers to the increase in pledged loans minus the decrease. This metric helps in understanding the company's financing activities and its asset-liability situation.</p>
