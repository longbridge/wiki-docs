# Accounts Payable 

<ArticleMeta id={101101} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Accounts payable (AP), or "payables," refer to a company's short-term obligations owed to its creditors or suppliers, which have not yet been paid. Payables appear on a company's balance sheet as a current liability.Another, less common usage of "AP," refers to the business department or division that is responsible for making payments owed by the company to suppliers and other creditors.Accounts payable can be compared with accounts receivable.
