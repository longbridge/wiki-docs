# Nominal Yield

<ArticleMeta id={101534} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:58'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A bond's nominal yield, depicted as a percentage, is calculated by dividing total interest paid annually by the face, or par, value of the bond. It represents the annual interest income that investors can receive based on the bond's face value, without taking into account market price fluctuations or the effects of inflation.</p>
