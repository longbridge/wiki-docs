# M1

<ArticleMeta id={100048} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

M1 is the money supply composed of currency in circulation, demand deposits, and other liquid deposits (including savings deposits). M1 represents the most liquid portion of the money supply as it includes currency and assets that can be quickly converted into cash. It is also known as narrow money supply.
