# Incremental Analysis

<ArticleMeta id={101155} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Incremental Analysis is a decision-making tool used to compare the costs and benefits of different decision options to determine which option will provide the greatest net benefit or the smallest net cost. The core of incremental analysis is to focus on the changes, i.e., incremental costs and incremental benefits, rather than the overall costs and benefits.</p>
