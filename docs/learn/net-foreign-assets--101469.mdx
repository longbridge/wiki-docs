# Net Foreign Assets 

<ArticleMeta id={101469} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Net foreign assets (NFA) determine whether a country is a creditor or debtor nation by measuring the difference in its external assets and liabilities.
