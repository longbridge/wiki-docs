# Simple Random Sample

<ArticleMeta id={101975} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:58'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

A simple random sample is a subset of a statistical population in which each member of the subset has an equal probability of being chosen. A simple random sample is meant to be an unbiased representation of a group.

This method is suitable for smaller populations.</li><li><strong>Without Replacement:</strong> After each selection, the chosen individual is removed from the population, ensuring that they cannot be selected again.
