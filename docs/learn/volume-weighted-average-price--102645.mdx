# Volume-Weighted Average Price 

<ArticleMeta id={102645} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 00:21:27'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The volume-weighted average price (VWAP) is a technical analysis indicator used on intraday charts that resets at the start of every new trading session. It's the average price a security has traded at throughout the day, based on both volume and price.</p><p>VWAP is important because it provides traders with insight into both the price trend and value of a security.</p>
