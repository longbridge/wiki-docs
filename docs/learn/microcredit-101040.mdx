# Microcredit

<ArticleMeta id={101040} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:01:49'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Microcredit is a common form of microfinance that involves an extremely small loan given to an individual to help them become self-employed or grow a small business. Microcredit is&nbsp;also known as "microlending" or "microloan."</p>
