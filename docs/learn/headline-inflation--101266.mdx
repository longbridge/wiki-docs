# Headline Inflation 

<ArticleMeta id={101266} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Headline inflation is the overall inflation in an economy, referring to the inflation data of a basket of goods that includes commodities such as food and energy. This is different from core inflation, which excludes food and energy prices when calculating inflation.</p>
