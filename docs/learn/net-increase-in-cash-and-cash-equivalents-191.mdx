# Net Increase in Cash and Cash Equivalents

<ArticleMeta id={191} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:02:05'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Net increase in cash and cash equivalents is an important indicator in the cash flow statement of a company, which reflects the net increase of cash and cash equivalents within a certain period. This indicator can be used to evaluate whether the company's operating activities can generate sufficient cash flow to support the company's development and debt repayment.
