# Forward Rate

<ArticleMeta id={101146} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The forward rate is the interest rate applicable to a specific future period. It is derived from the current spot rates. Forward rates are commonly used in financial markets for forward contracts and futures contracts to lock in future interest rates, thereby reducing the uncertainty caused by interest rate fluctuations. The calculation of forward rates is primarily based on the principle of no arbitrage, which involves deriving the future interest rate from the current spot rates and the term structure.</p>
