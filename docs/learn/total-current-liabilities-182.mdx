# Total Current Liabilities

<ArticleMeta id={182} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:02:46'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Total current liabilities refer to the sum of all current liabilities of a company. Current liabilities are debts that a company will repay within one year or within one operating cycle exceeding one year. In theory, current liabilities and current assets are closely related, and a comparison between the two can provide a rough understanding of a company's short-term solvency and liquidation ability.</p>
