# White Knight

<ArticleMeta id={100239} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A White Knight is a term used in mergers and acquisitions to describe a friendly acquirer who comes to the rescue of a target company facing a hostile takeover. The White Knight typically offers a higher price or more favorable terms than the hostile bidder, thereby helping the target company avoid being taken over by the hostile party. The intervention of a White Knight is generally seen as beneficial to the target company's management and shareholders, as it can provide better acquisition terms and protect the company's long-term interests.</p>
