# Operational Efficiency

<ArticleMeta id={101150} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Operational Efficiency refers to an organization's ability to maximize output and benefits with the least amount of input through optimal resource allocation and process management. Improving operational efficiency not only reduces costs but also enhances productivity and competitiveness, leading to higher profits and market share for the organization.</p>
