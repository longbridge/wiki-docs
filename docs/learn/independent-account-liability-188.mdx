# Independent account liability

<ArticleMeta id={188} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:02:19'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Independent account liability refers to the liabilities held by investors or institutions in independent accounts opened with custodian banks. Independent account liabilities usually include fixed income investment instruments, stocks, and cash, etc.
