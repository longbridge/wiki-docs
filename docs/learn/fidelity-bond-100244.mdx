# Fidelity Bond

<ArticleMeta id={100244} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A fidelity bond is an insurance product designed to protect a company from financial losses due to fraudulent, dishonest, or theft-related actions committed by its employees. It typically covers a range of misconduct that employees might engage in while performing their duties. Fidelity bonds are a part of a company's risk management strategy, especially for positions that involve handling cash, financial transactions, or sensitive information. By purchasing this insurance, a company can receive financial compensation in the event of employee dishonesty, thereby mitigating potential financial losses.</p>
