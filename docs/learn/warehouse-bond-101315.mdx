# Warehouse Bond

<ArticleMeta id={101315} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 10:49:06'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Warehouse Bond is a financial instrument commonly used in the mortgage industry. It is a short-term loan provided by a financial institution (such as a bank) to a mortgage company, allowing the mortgage company to fund mortgages that are intended to be sold to investors. The term "warehouse" refers to the concept of temporarily "warehousing" these mortgages on the financial institution's books before they are sold to investors.</p>
