# Development Expenditure

<ArticleMeta id={128} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:05:25'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Development expenditure refers to expenses incurred by a company for the development of new products, technologies, or markets, etc. Development expenditure is typically a long-term investment, as companies hope to obtain long-term economic benefits through the development of new products or technologies.
