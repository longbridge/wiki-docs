# Northbound Funds

<ArticleMeta id={295} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:56:16'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>"Northbound Funds" refers to the capital flowing from Hong Kong into mainland China's stock markets via the Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect or Shenzhen-Hong Kong Stock Connect. These funds are usually composed of international investors and local Hong Kong investors who invest in stocks listed on the Shanghai and Shenzhen stock exchanges.</p>
