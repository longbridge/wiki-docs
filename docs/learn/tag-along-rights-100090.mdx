# Tag-Along Rights

<ArticleMeta id={100090} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:50:46'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(17,17,17)"}}>Tag-along rights act as a protective shield for minority shareholders. They allow these smaller stakeholders to join—or "tag along"—when a majority shareholder sells their stake, ensuring they can exit on the same terms. On the flip side, drag-along rights empower majority shareholders to force—or "drag"—minority shareholders into a sale, potentially against their wishes.</span></p>
