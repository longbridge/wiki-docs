# Impulse Wave Pattern

<ArticleMeta id={102043} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

An impulse wave pattern is an indication of a strong move in a financial asset's price coinciding with the main direction of the underlying trend. Impulse waves can refer to upward movements in uptrends or downward movements in downtrends.The term is used frequently by adherents of Elliott Wave theory, a method for analyzing and predicting price movements in the financial markets.
