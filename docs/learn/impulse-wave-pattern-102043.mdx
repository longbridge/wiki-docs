<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>The Impulse Wave Pattern in financial market technical analysis is used to identify strong price movements that are in line with the primary trend direction. This pattern manifests as price increases in uptrends and price decreases in downtrends and is a key component in Elliott Wave theory for predicting market trends.</span></p>
