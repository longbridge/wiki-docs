# Debt Restructuring

<ArticleMeta id={101175} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Debt Restructuring refers to the process of renegotiating the terms of debt between a borrower and creditors to enable the borrower to better fulfill their debt obligations. Methods of restructuring may include extending the repayment period, lowering interest rates, reducing the principal amount, or converting debt into equity. The goal of debt restructuring is to help the borrower regain financial stability while allowing creditors to recover as much of the owed amount as possible.</p>
