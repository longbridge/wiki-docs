# Interest Payment

<ArticleMeta id={103212} updatedAt={'2023-10-03 08:20:23'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Interest payment refers to the payment of interest by a borrower to a lender. Interest payments are a financial cost for the company and are usually made periodically, such as quarterly or annually. The amount of interest payment depends on the loan amount and the interest rate.</p>
