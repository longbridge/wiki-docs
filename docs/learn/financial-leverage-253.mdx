# Financial Leverage

<ArticleMeta id={253} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:59:24'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Financial leverage refers to a financial strategy in which a company uses borrowed funds to invest in or engage in business activities in order to increase investment return or profitability. Financial leverage can be achieved through borrowing, financing, issuing bonds, and other means.
