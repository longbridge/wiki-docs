# SSE, SZSE, and HKEX

<ArticleMeta id={176} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:02:58'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

The Shanghai and Shenzhen-Hong Kong Stock Exchanges refer to the three main stock exchanges in China, namely the Shanghai Stock Exchange, Shenzhen Stock Exchange, and Hong Kong Stock Exchange. These three exchanges are the major securities markets in Mainland China and Hong Kong, responsible for stock issuance, trading, and regulation in Mainland China and Hong Kong respectively.
