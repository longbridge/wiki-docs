# Unrealized Investment Loss

<ArticleMeta id={168} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:03:14'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Unrealized Investment Loss refers to the decrease in the market value of an investor's holdings (such as stocks, bonds, funds, etc.) that has not yet been sold, and therefore, the loss has not been actually realized. This loss is only reflected on paper, and the actual loss will only be incurred when the asset is sold. This concept helps investors understand the potential risks and current market performance of their investment portfolio.</p>
