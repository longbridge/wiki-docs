# Incidental Expenses 

<ArticleMeta id={100448} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Incidental Expenses, also known as miscellaneous expenses or ancillary expenses, refer to additional costs incurred during the course of primary activities or business operations. These expenses are not part of the main costs but are still closely related to the conduct of business. Common incidental expenses include travel expenses, office supplies, entertainment expenses, and communication costs. Although these expenses might be relatively small individually, they can accumulate and impact the financial health of a business. Companies need to consider and manage incidental expenses effectively in their financial management and budgeting processes.</p>
