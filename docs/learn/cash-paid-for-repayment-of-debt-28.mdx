<p>Cash paid for repayment of debt refers to the cash outflows by a company or individual to fulfill their debt obligations. These debts can include loans, bonds, accounts payable, etc. Cash paid for repayment of debt is typically reported in the "financing activities" section of the cash flow statement because it directly relates to the company's financing activities.</p>
