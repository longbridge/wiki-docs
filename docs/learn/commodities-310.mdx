# Commodities

<ArticleMeta id={310} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:55:38'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Commodities refer to goods with unified quality standards, large trading volumes, and large price fluctuations. Commodities can be divided into energy (such as crude oil, natural gas), metals (such as iron ore, copper), agricultural products (such as soybeans, wheat), etc. Investors can participate in commodity investment through futures markets, spot markets, or funds.
