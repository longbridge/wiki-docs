# Dividend Yield

<ArticleMeta id={103020} updatedAt={'2023-09-29 02:20:23'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Dividend yield refers to the ratio of the dividends distributed per share by a company to the current price of the stock. It is an important metric for investors to gauge the cash return they receive from holding a stock. A high dividend yield typically indicates a company with stable cash flows and good profitability.</p>
