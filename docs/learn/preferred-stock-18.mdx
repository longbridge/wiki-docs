# Preferred stock

<ArticleMeta id={18} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:10:51'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Preferred stock capital refers to the funds raised by a company through the issuance of preferred shares. These shares are a hybrid form of financing that combines elements of both equity and debt, offering fixed dividend payments and a higher claim on assets in the event of liquidation compared to common stock. Preferred stock capital is often used to strengthen a company's capital structure and reduce financial risk while providing investors with a stable income stream.</p>
