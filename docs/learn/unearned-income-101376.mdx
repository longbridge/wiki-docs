# Unearned Income

<ArticleMeta id={101376} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Unearned Income, also known as Passive Income, refers to income that individuals or businesses earn through means other than direct labor or active business activities. It can encompass rental income, dividends, interest, royalties, licensing fees, capital gains (such as profits from the appreciation of investments in stocks, real estate, etc.).</p>
