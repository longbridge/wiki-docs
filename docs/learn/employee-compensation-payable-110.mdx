# Employee Compensation Payable

<ArticleMeta id={110} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:06:15'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Employee compensation payable refers to the wages, bonuses, allowances, subsidies and other salary benefits that a company should pay to its employees. It includes various types of salary payments such as basic salary, performance-based pay, allowances, bonuses, and welfare benefits.
