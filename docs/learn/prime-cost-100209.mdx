# Prime Cost

<ArticleMeta id={100209} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Prime costs are a firm's expenses directly related to the materials and labor used in production.Â It refers to a manufactured product's costs, which are calculated to ensure the best profit margin for a company. The prime cost calculates the direct costs of raw materials and labor that are involved in the production of a good. Direct costs do not include indirect expenses, such as advertising and administrative costs.
