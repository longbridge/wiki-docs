# Public Listing Strategy

<ArticleMeta id={103749} updatedAt={'2023-10-12 02:22:04'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>An public listing strategy refers to a company's plan to go public through an initial public offering (IPO) or other methods of listing on a stock exchange. This strategy involves selecting the appropriate market, pricing, issuance size, and timing. An effective public listing strategy helps a company raise capital, enhance brand visibility, and improve market competitiveness.</p>
