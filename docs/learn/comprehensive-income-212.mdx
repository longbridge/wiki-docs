# Comprehensive Income

<ArticleMeta id={212} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:01:03'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Comprehensive income refers to the total income of a company over a specific accounting period, encompassing both net income and other comprehensive income. Net income is the remaining part of the company's main business revenue after deducting costs and expenses, while other comprehensive income includes items not reflected in the income statement but directly recorded in shareholders' equity, such as foreign currency translation adjustments, changes in the fair value of available-for-sale financial assets, and the effective portion of cash flow hedges. Comprehensive income reflects the overall changes in shareholders' equity during a specific period.</p>
