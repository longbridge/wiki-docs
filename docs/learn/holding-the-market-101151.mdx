# Holding The Market

<ArticleMeta id={101151} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Holding The Market refers to the actions taken by large funds or institutional investors in the stock market to control the price of a stock by buying or selling large quantities of it. The purpose of holding the market is to maintain the stock price at a certain level, prevent significant price fluctuations, or to manipulate the price for greater profits at a future time.</p>
