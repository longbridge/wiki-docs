# Five-Year Rule

<ArticleMeta id={101012} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Five-Year Rule refers to a condition that must be met when withdrawing funds from certain retirement accounts or investment plans, requiring that the funds must have been held in the account for at least five years to be tax-free or to avoid penalties. This rule is intended to encourage long-term investment and savings, ensuring that account holders do not withdraw funds prematurely.</p>
