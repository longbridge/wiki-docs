# Blended Rate

<ArticleMeta id={100591} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:51'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Blended Rate is an average interest rate derived from combining multiple different rates. It is commonly used in loans or investment portfolios where multiple rates are involved, simplifying the calculation and comparison of interest. For instance, in a mortgage scenario, if a borrower consolidates an existing high-interest loan with a new lower-interest loan, the blended rate reflects the overall interest level of the combined loans. The blended rate is typically calculated by taking a weighted average of the individual rates based on their respective weights. This concept is crucial in financial decision-making, enabling borrowers and investors to better assess the overall cost or return.</p>
