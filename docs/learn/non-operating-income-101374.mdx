# Non-Operating Income

<ArticleMeta id={101374} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Non-operating income is the portion of an organization's&nbsp;income&nbsp;that is derived from activities not related to its core business operations. It can include items such as dividend income, profits, or losses from investments, as well as gains or losses incurred by foreign exchange and asset write-downs.&nbsp;</p>
