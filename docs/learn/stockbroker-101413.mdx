# Stockbroker

<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A stockbroker is a professional who buys and sells stocks and other securities on behalf of clients in the securities market. Stockbrokers typically work for brokerage firms or investment companies, providing investment advice, executing trades, and managing client accounts to help investors achieve their financial goals.</p>
