# Interest Receivable

<ArticleMeta id={118} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:05:42'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Interest Receivable refers to the amounts a company is owed for interest income that has been earned but not yet received. These amounts are recorded as receivables on the company's financial statements and represent future cash inflows.</p>
