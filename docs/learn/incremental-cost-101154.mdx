# Incremental Cost

<ArticleMeta id={101154} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Incremental Cost refers to the total additional cost incurred when producing or providing one more unit of product or service. Incremental cost is commonly used in decision analysis to help businesses evaluate the economic benefits of producing additional products or services. It is a specific application of marginal cost, reflecting the impact of changes in production volume on total cost.</p>
