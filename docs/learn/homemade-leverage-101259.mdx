# Homemade Leverage

<ArticleMeta id={101259} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Homemade leverage is a financial concept that posits that as long as investors borrow under the same conditions as the company, they can artificially replicate the effects of corporate leverage. When individuals invest in an unleveraged company, they can use homemade leverage to recreate the leverage effect, including taking personal loans for their investments.</p>
