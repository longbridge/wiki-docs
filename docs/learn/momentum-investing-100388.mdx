# Momentum Investing

<ArticleMeta id={100388} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Momentum investing is a strategy that aims to capitalize on the continuance of an existing market trend. It is a trading strategy in which investors buy securities that are already rising and look to sell them when they look to have peakedMomentum, in markets, refers to the capacity for a price trend to sustain itself going forward.
