# Reversal Factor

<ArticleMeta id={321} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:19'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The reversal factor refers to the phenomenon where asset prices tend to revert to their mean after experiencing periods of gains or losses. Investors can use the reversal factor to buy or sell when prices deviate from the mean, aiming to profit from the corrections. The reversal factor is widely used in quantitative investment strategies.</p>
