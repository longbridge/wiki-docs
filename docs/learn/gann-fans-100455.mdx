# Gann Fan

<ArticleMeta id={100455} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:32:44'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Gann Fan is a technical analysis tool developed by the renowned financial analyst William D. Gann. It is used to predict future price movements in the market. The Gann Fan consists of a series of angle lines that extend outward from significant price points (such as highs or lows) at specific angles (like 1x1, 1x2, 2x1, etc.). Each angle line represents a relationship between price and time, helping traders identify potential support and resistance levels.</p>
