# Non-operating Expenses

<ArticleMeta id={232} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:00:18'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Non-operating Expenses refer to the costs incurred by a company from activities outside its primary business operations. These expenses are typically non-recurring and irregular, and may include losses from the disposal of fixed assets, investment losses, charitable donations, fines, and foreign exchange losses. While non-operating expenses are not related to the core business activities, they can still impact the overall financial health and profitability of the company.</p>
