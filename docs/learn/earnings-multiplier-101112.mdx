# Earnings Multiplier

<ArticleMeta id={101112} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 10:58:36'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The earnings multiplier is a financial metric that frames a company's current stock price in terms of the company's earnings per share&nbsp;(EPS) of stock, that's simply computed as price per share/earnings per share. The earnings multiplier can be used as a simplified valuation tool with which to compare the&nbsp;relative costliness of the stocks of similar companies. It can likewise help investors judge current stock prices against their historical prices on an earnings-relative basis.</p>
