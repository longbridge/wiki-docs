# Zoning Ordinance

<ArticleMeta id={100431} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A zoning ordinance is a law or regulation enacted by a government to manage and regulate land use. The primary purpose of zoning ordinances is to ensure the rational use of land, protect public interests, and promote urban planning and development by dividing land into different use zones (e.g., residential, commercial, industrial).</p>
