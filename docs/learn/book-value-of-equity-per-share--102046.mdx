# Book Value Of Equity Per Share 

<ArticleMeta id={102046} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:59:00'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>The Book Value of Equity Per Share (BVPS) is calculated by dividing the total equity of a company, which is the net value after subtracting total liabilities from total assets, by the number of common shares outstanding. It serves as a measure of the intrinsic value of a company's stock and is commonly used for investment analysis and valuation.</span></p>
