# Adhesion Contract

<ArticleMeta id={100794} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:51'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

An adhesionÂ contract is an agreement that usually has non-negotiable terms and conditions. Generally, it's prepared by a party to a potential transaction that has the product or service sought by another party, the consumer. The former has the stronger bargaining position. The latter must accept the adhesion contract to obtain the product or service.Adhesion contracts are also known as standard, standardized, or boilerplate contracts.
