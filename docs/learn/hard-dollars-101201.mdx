# Hard Dollars

<ArticleMeta id={101201} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Hard Dollars refer to actual cash payments made by investors or institutions in the financial industry. These payments are typically used to pay for services provided by third parties, such as research reports, trading commissions, consulting services, and more. Hard dollars are different from soft dollars, which are paid through trading volume or other non-direct payment forms.&nbsp;</p>
