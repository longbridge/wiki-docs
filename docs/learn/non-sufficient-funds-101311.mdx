# Non-Sufficient Funds

<ArticleMeta id={101311} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 19:49:18'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Non-Sufficient Funds (NSF) refers to a situation where a bank account does not have enough money to cover a transaction. When the account holder attempts to make a payment or withdrawal that exceeds the available balance, the bank will decline the transaction and may charge an NSF fee. This often occurs with check payments, automatic debits, or credit card payments. Frequent NSF occurrences can lead to additional fees, penalties, and potentially harm the account holder's credit record.</p>
