# Graduated Lease

<ArticleMeta id={100430} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A graduated lease, also known as a step lease or graded lease, is a leasing agreement in which the rent increases at predetermined intervals over the lease term. This type of lease is commonly used in commercial leasing contracts, such as for office spaces, retail spaces, and industrial properties. The graduated lease is designed to accommodate the growth and cash flow variations of the tenant, allowing them to pay lower rent initially and gradually increasing the rent as their business grows and their income increases.</p>
