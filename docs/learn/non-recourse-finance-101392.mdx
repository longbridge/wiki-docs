# Non-Recourse Finance

<ArticleMeta id={101392} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Non-Recourse Finance is a type of loan arrangement where the lender's recovery is limited to the specific collateral provided by the borrower and cannot pursue the borrower for any shortfall beyond the value of the collateral. In this arrangement, if the borrower defaults, the lender can only seize the collateral asset and cannot claim other assets or income of the borrower to recover the loan. This type of financing is commonly used in project finance and real estate investments.</p>
