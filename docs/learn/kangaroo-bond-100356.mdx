# Kangaroo Bond

<ArticleMeta id={100356} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Kangaroo Bond is a type of bond issued by a non-Australian company or entity in the Australian market, denominated in Australian dollars (AUD). These bonds are typically issued by foreign corporations, governments, or international organizations to raise funds in Australian dollars. Issuers of Kangaroo Bonds can tap into the Australian capital market and investor base, while investors can diversify their portfolios and gain exposure to AUD-denominated assets. The issuance and trading of Kangaroo Bonds are subject to regulation by Australian financial markets.</p>
