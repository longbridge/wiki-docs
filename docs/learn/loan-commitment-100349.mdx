# Loan Commitment

<ArticleMeta id={100349} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A loan commitment is an agreement by a bank or financial institution to provide a borrower with a loan at a future date or under specific conditions. Loan commitments can be either conditional (Conditional Commitment) or unconditional (Unconditional Commitment). In a conditional loan commitment, the borrower must meet certain conditions (such as providing collateral or meeting credit standards) to receive the loan. In an unconditional loan commitment, the bank commits to providing the loan when the borrower applies. A loan commitment typically includes the loan amount, interest rate, term, and other relevant terms. It provides the borrower with certainty regarding the availability of funds, aiding in financial planning.</p>
