# Operating Cycle

<ArticleMeta id={231} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:00:34'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Operating cycle refers to the time period that a company goes through from purchasing raw materials, producing products, selling products to receiving payment. It reflects the time and process required by a company within one production and sales cycle. The length of the operating cycle may vary among different industries and companies.
