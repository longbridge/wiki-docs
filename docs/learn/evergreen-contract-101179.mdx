# Evergreen Contract

<ArticleMeta id={101179} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 19:55:30'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Evergreen Contract refers to a contract that automatically renews itself without a specified end date. Unless one party provides notice of termination within the period stipulated in the contract, the contract remains in effect and renews automatically. These types of contracts are often used in service agreements, lease agreements, or long-term supply agreements, helping to simplify contract management and avoid service interruptions after the contract's expiration.</p>
