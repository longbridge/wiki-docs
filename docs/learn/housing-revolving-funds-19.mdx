# Housing Revolving Funds

<ArticleMeta id={19} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:09:52'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Housing revolving funds refer to housing funds obtained by enterprises from various prescribed sources for various housing expenses of employees, excluding public welfare funds, housing depreciation and housing provident funds. In accounting treatment, it is both a component of the off-balance sheet item "housing fund" and a component of the balance sheet.</p>
