# High-Low Index

<ArticleMeta id={100464} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:32:17'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The High-Low Index is a technical analysis indicator used to measure market sentiment and trends. Specifically, the High-Low Index is the ratio of the number of stocks making new 52-week highs to the total number of stocks making new 52-week highs and lows. When the High-Low Index is close to 1, it indicates a strong market with more stocks making new highs. Conversely, when the High-Low Index is close to 0, it indicates a weak market with more stocks making new lows.&nbsp;</p>
