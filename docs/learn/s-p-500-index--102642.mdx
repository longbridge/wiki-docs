# S&P 500 Index 

<ArticleMeta id={102642} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 15:22:18'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The S&amp;P 500 Index, or Standard &amp; Poor's 500 Index, is a market-capitalization-weighted index of 500 leading&nbsp;publicly traded companies in the U.S.&nbsp;The index actually has 503 components because three of them have two share classes listed.</p><p>It is not an exact list of the top 500 U.S. companies by market cap because there are other criteria that the index includes. Still, the S&amp;P 500 index&nbsp;is regarded as one of the best gauges of prominent American equities' performance, and by extension, that of the stock market overall.</p>
