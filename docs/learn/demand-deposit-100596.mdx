# Demand Deposit

<ArticleMeta id={100596} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:51'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A demand deposit account (DDA) is a bank account from which deposited funds can be withdrawn at any time, without advance notice.&nbsp;</p>
