# Contingent Value Rights

<ArticleMeta id={100032} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:53:19'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The term contingent value right (CVR) refers to a right often granted to shareholders of a company facing restructuring or a buyout. These rights ensure that the shareholders get certain benefits if a specific event occurs, usually within a specified time frame.&nbsp;</p><p>These rights are&nbsp;similar to&nbsp;options because&nbsp;they frequently have an expiration date,&nbsp;beyond which the rights to the additional benefits will not apply. CVRs are usually related to the performance of a company's stock.</p>
