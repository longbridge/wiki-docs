# Net Increase in Funds Borrowed

<ArticleMeta id={154} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:04:55'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

The net increase in funds borrowed refers to the difference between the amount of funds borrowed by a company or institution and the net increase in funds borrowed by the company or institution during a certain period. The net increase in funds borrowed can reflect the flow of funds and the growth rate of funds borrowed by a company or institution.
