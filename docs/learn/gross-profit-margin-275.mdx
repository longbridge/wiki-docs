# Gross Profit Margin

<ArticleMeta id={275} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:57:39'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The gross profit margin is the percentage of sales revenue that exceeds the cost of goods sold (COGS). It reflects the profitability of a company's products or services and is a key indicator of operational efficiency. A higher gross profit margin typically indicates strong pricing power and effective cost control, while a lower gross profit margin may suggest significant competitive pressure in the market.</p>
