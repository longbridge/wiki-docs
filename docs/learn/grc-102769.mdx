# Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance

<ArticleMeta id={102769} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:24:37'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance (GRC) is an integrated framework that organizations use to manage and control their business activities. Governance involves decision-making and control structures within the company, risk management focuses on identifying and addressing potential risks, and compliance ensures adherence to laws, regulations, and internal policies. The GRC framework aims to enhance transparency, efficiency, and accountability within the organization.</p>
