# Headline Risk

<ArticleMeta id={100268} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Headline Risk refers to the potential negative impact on a company's, industry's, or market's reputation and performance due to media coverage, particularly negative news. This type of risk is often associated with unexpected events, scandals, legal issues, product recalls, or other negative news that can attract public attention. Headline risk can lead to investor sentiment fluctuations, which in turn can affect a company's stock price and market value. Strategies for managing headline risk include proactive public relations management, transparent information disclosure, and having a crisis management plan in place.</p>
