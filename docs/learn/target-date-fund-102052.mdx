# Target-Date Fund

<ArticleMeta id={102052} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>A Target-Date Fund is an investment fund product designed to meet an investor's financial needs at a specific point in time, such as retirement. These funds typically adopt a more aggressive investment strategy in the early years of investment and gradually shift towards more stable and conservative asset allocation as the target date approaches, to reduce risk and protect accumulated earnings. Target-Date Funds are suitable for long-term investment planning, especially for investors saving for retirement.</span></p>
