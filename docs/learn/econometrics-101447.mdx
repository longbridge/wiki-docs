# Econometrics

<ArticleMeta id={101447} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Econometrics is a branch of economics that combines mathematics, statistics, and economic theory to analyze economic data and develop and test economic models. The main objectives of econometrics are to empirically test economic theories, forecast economic variables, and evaluate policies. Key methods in econometrics include regression analysis, time series analysis, and panel data analysis.</p>
