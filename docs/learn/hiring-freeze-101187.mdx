# Hiring Freeze

<ArticleMeta id={101187} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A hiring freeze is when an employer stops hiring workers, usually temporarily, in an effort to contain costs. Such cost-cutting may be the result of financial distress, but even large, successful companies may opt to pause hiring amid an economic slowdown, recession or instances of overcapacity.</p>
