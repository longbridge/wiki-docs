# Wasting Trust

<ArticleMeta id={100374} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Wasting Trust is a type of trust structure where the trust assets are gradually depleted or distributed over a specified period until the trust assets are entirely exhausted. Typically, this type of trust will make scheduled payments of a fixed amount or a percentage of the trust assets to the beneficiaries over a set period or until certain conditions are met. Wasting Trusts are often used in estate planning to provide beneficiaries with a stable income over a specific period while gradually reducing the trust's assets. By the end of the predetermined period, the trust assets will be fully distributed.</p>
