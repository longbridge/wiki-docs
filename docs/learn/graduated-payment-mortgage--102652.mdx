# Graduated Payment Mortgage 

<ArticleMeta id={102652} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:59:30'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A graduated payment mortgage (GPM)&nbsp;is a type of&nbsp;fixed-rate mortgage&nbsp;for which the&nbsp;payments&nbsp;increase gradually from an initial low base level to a higher final level. Typically, the payments will grow between 7% to 12% annually from their initial base payment amount until the full monthly payment amount is reached.</p>
