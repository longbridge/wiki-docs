# Total Asset Turnover

<ArticleMeta id={135} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:04:33'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Total asset turnover refers to the ability of a company to generate sales revenue using its total assets during a certain period of time. A higher total asset turnover indicates that the company has generated more sales revenue with the same total assets, while a lower total asset turnover indicates that the company is not fully utilizing its assets.
