# Owner Financing

<ArticleMeta id={101080} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Owner Financing refers to a situation where the seller provides a loan to the buyer for purchasing property, instead of the buyer obtaining a loan from a traditional bank or financial institution. The buyer pays a down payment and makes regular payments to the seller as agreed.</p>
