# Non-Operating Net Income

<ArticleMeta id={287} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:56:42'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Non-operating net income refers to the net income obtained by a company in non-operating activities. Non-operating activities include investment income, subsidy income, income from asset disposal, etc. This indicator reflects the income obtained by a company in non-core business and can be used to evaluate the company's diversification ability and income stability.
