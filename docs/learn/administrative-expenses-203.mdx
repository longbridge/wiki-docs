# Administrative Expenses

<ArticleMeta id={203} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:01:35'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Administrative expenses refer to expenses incurred by a company for the operation and support of daily operational activities. These expenses include salaries, office expenses, advertising expenses, lease expenses, etc. Administrative expenses are necessary expenses for the normal operation of a company, but excessive administrative expenses may affect the company's profitability.
