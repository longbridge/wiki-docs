# Cash Outflows from Investing Activities

<ArticleMeta id={146} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:04:14'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cash outflows from investing activities refer to the cash spent by a company on its investing activities over a specific period. These activities typically include the purchase of fixed assets, intangible assets, investment properties, subsidiaries or business units, and securities such as stocks and bonds. This metric is a crucial part of the company's cash flow statement, indicating the cash expenditures related to investment activities.</p>
