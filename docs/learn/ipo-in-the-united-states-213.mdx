# IPO in the United States

<ArticleMeta id={213} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:59'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

In the United States, IPO refers to the process of a company's initial public offering of stocks. In the US, the initial public offering of stocks is a common financing method. By transforming a private company into a publicly listed company, the company can attract more investors and raise more funds to support business development. The US IPO market is usually very active and attracts a lot of attention and participation from investors.
