# Net operating cycle

<ArticleMeta id={53} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:08:20'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Net operating cycle refers to the average time it takes for a company to complete one production and business activity. The net operating cycle includes the entire process from raw material procurement to product sales receipt. It is an important indicator for measuring the operational efficiency and capital turnover ability of a company. Generally speaking, the shorter the net operating cycle, the higher the operational efficiency of the company.
