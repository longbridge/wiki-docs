# High-Frequency Trading 

<ArticleMeta id={100466} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>High-Frequency Trading (HFT) is a trading strategy that uses complex algorithms and high-speed computers to execute a large number of trades in extremely short periods. HFT typically involves markets such as stocks, futures, and forex. High-frequency traders exploit tiny price discrepancies to make profits through rapid buying and selling.</p><p>The advantages of HFT include increased market liquidity and reduced bid-ask spreads. However, it is also controversial, as it may contribute to market volatility and create an uneven playing field for regular investors.</p>
