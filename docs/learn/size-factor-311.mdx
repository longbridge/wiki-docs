# Size Factor

<ArticleMeta id={311} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:34'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The size factor, also known as the market capitalization factor, is a component in asset pricing models that uses a company's market value to explain its expected returns. Typically, small-cap companies are expected to yield higher returns than large-cap companies due to higher risk and growth potential.</p>
