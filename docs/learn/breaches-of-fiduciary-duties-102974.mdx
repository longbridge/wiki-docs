# breaches of fiduciary duties

<ArticleMeta id={102974} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 14:20:30'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Breach of fiduciary duty refers to the behavior of a trustee who fails to act in the best interests of the settlor or fails to fulfill their fiduciary responsibilities in a situation where there is a relationship between the trustee and the settlor. Breach of fiduciary duty may include conflicts of interest, abuse of power, failure to fulfill supervisory duties, etc.
