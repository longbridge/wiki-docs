# Buying Power

<ArticleMeta id={100489} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Buying power is the money an investor has available to buy securities in a trading context. Buying power equals the total cash held in the brokerage account plus all available margin.</p>
