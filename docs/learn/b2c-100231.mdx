# B2C

<ArticleMeta id={100231} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:44:57'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>B2C (Business to Consumer) is a business model where companies sell products or services directly to individual consumers. This model typically operates through online or offline retail channels and aims to meet the needs of end consumers directly. B2C is particularly prevalent in the e-commerce sector, where businesses sell goods or provide services to individual customers via their own websites, third-party platforms (such as Amazon, Alibaba, or eBay), or physical stores.</p>
