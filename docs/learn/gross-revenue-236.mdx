# Gross Revenue

<ArticleMeta id={236} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:00:05'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Gross Revenue is the total income obtained by a company through the sale of goods or the provision of services during a certain accounting period. It includes main operating revenue and other operating revenue. Total operating revenue is an important indicator for measuring the company's business performance, reflecting its sales ability and market competitiveness.</p>
