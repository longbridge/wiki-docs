# Hot Hand

<ArticleMeta id={100391} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Hot Hand phenomenon refers to the belief that a person who has experienced success in a random event has a greater chance of further success in additional attempts. This effect is commonly observed in sports, such as a basketball player making several consecutive shots, leading observers to believe that the player is more likely to make the next shot as well.</p>
