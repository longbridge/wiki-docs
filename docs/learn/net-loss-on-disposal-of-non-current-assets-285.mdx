# Net Loss on Disposal of Non-Current Assets

<ArticleMeta id={285} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:57:07'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Net loss on disposal of non-current assets refers to the loss incurred by a company when it sells, scraps, or otherwise disposes of non-current assets such as fixed assets, intangible assets, or long-term investments. Non-current assets are typically those that are not easily convertible to cash and have a useful life of more than one year. When these assets are disposed of at a price lower than their book value, a net loss is recorded. This net loss is usually listed separately in financial statements to provide a clearer picture of the company's asset management and financial health.</p>
