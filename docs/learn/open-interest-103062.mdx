# open interest

<ArticleMeta id={103062} updatedAt={'2023-09-29 08:20:40'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Opening position refers to the number of futures contracts that have not been closed at a certain point in time, that is, the opening position is the number of future contracts that have not been closed in the futures market. Opening position can be used to measure market sentiment and expectations, as well as to evaluate market depth and liquidity.
