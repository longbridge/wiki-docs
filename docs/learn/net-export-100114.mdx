# Net Exports

<ArticleMeta id={100114} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Net exports are a measure of a nation's total trade. The formula for net exports is a simple one: The value of a nation's total export goods and services minus the value of all the goods and services it imports equals its net exports.</p><p>A nation that has positive net exports enjoys a trade surplus, while negative net exports indicate that the nation has&nbsp;a trade deficit. A nation's net exports are thus a component of its overall balance of trade.</p>
