# Operating Profit Margin

<ArticleMeta id={230} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:33'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Operating profit margin refers to the ratio between a company's operating profit and its operating income. It is one of the important indicators for evaluating a company's business condition and profitability. A higher operating profit margin indicates that a company earns a larger proportion of profit from selling products or providing services.
