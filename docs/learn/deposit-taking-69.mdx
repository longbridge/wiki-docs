# Deposit-taking

<ArticleMeta id={69} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:08:12'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Deposit-taking refers to the process by which banks or other financial institutions accept funds from customers in the form of deposits. These deposits can take various forms, such as demand deposits, time deposits, and savings deposits. By taking deposits, financial institutions obtain a source of funds that can be used for lending, investing, and other financial activities.</p>
