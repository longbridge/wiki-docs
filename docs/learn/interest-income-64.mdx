# Interest income

<ArticleMeta id={64} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:08:25'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Interest income is the interest earnings obtained by a company from the debts held by creditors. This is the fee that the company receives from creditors as a return on debt.
