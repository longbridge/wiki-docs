<p>Investment bank refers to financial institutions engaged in securities issuance, underwriting, trading, and investment banking business. Investment banks provide professional services for companies to raise capital in the capital market, including IPO issuances, bond issuances, equity financing, and more. Investment banks also participate in corporate restructuring, mergers and acquisitions, asset restructuring, and provide related consulting and risk management services.</p>
