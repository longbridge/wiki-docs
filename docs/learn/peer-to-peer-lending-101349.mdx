# Peer-To-Peer Lending

<ArticleMeta id={101349} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 10:47:34'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Peer-to-Peer Lending (P2P Lending) is a financial model that connects borrowers and investors directly through an online platform. The P2P lending platform acts as an intermediary, facilitating the process by which borrowers can obtain funds and investors can find investment opportunities. Borrowers can be individuals or businesses, commonly seeking personal loans or small business loans. P2P lending typically offers lower interest rates than traditional bank loans but comes with higher risks, as borrowers may not undergo stringent credit checks.&nbsp;</p>
