# Cost Accounting

<ArticleMeta id={100307} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cost accounting is a specialized branch of accounting focused on recording, analyzing, and controlling the costs associated with a business's production and operations. By collecting and categorizing cost data related to production and operations, it helps management understand the cost structure of the production process, enabling more effective decision-making. Cost accounting involves the allocation of direct and indirect costs, cost control and budgeting, cost analysis, and reporting. Its primary objective is to enhance cost efficiency, optimize resource allocation, and increase competitiveness.</p>
