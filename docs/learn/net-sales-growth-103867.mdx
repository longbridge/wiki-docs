# Net Sales Growth

<ArticleMeta id={103867} updatedAt={'2023-10-13 08:20:36'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Net sales growth refers to the percentage increase in a company's net sales over a specific period compared to a previous period. Net sales are the total sales revenue minus sales discounts, returns, and allowances. Net sales growth is a key indicator of a company's sales performance and market competitiveness, reflecting its ability to grow and expand in the market.</p>
