# Interbank Deposit

<ArticleMeta id={84} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:06:48'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Interbank deposits refer to funds that a company deposits with other financial institutions. When managing and investing funds, a company may choose to deposit a portion of its funds with other financial institutions to achieve better returns or diversify risks. Interbank deposits can be short-term deposits or long-term investments.
