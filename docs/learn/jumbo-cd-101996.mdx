# Jumbo CD

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>A Jumbo CD is a financial product that requires a higher minimum deposit than standard certificates of deposit. This higher threshold qualifies the Jumbo CD to offer a higher interest rate compared to traditional CDs. As a type of savings account, CDs pay interest at a fixed or variable rate, on the condition that depositors keep their funds in the account until the agreed maturity date.</span></p>
