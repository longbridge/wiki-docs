# Dividend Payout Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={223} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:49'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Dividend Payout Ratio is the proportion of a company's earnings that is paid out to shareholders as dividends. This ratio can be used to measure the company's ability to return to shareholders and the generosity of its dividend policy.</p>
