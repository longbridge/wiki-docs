# Underwriter

<ArticleMeta id={100063} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>In the process of securities issuance, an underwriter is a financial institution or intermediary that assists the issuer (such as a company or government) in arranging and selling securities. The main responsibilities of an underwriter include pricing, distribution, and providing market advice.</p>
