# Other operating expenses

<ArticleMeta id={46} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:09:46'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Other operating expenses are expenses incurred by a company in its operating activities that are unrelated to its main business. These expenses are usually special or non-recurring expenses, such as fines, donations, travel expenses, marketing expenses, etc.
