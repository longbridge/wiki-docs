# Weak Hands

<ArticleMeta id={100163} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:47:41'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>"Weak hands" is a term often used to describe traders and investors who lack conviction in their strategies or lack the resources to carry them out. It also refers to a futures trader who never intends to take or provide delivery of the underlying commodity or index.</p><p>Weak hands can be contrasted with strong hands, or "diamond hands."</p>
