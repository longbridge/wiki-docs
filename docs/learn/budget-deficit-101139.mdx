# Budget Deficit

<ArticleMeta id={101139} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A budget deficit occurs when a government's expenditures exceed its revenues within a fiscal year. In simple terms, it means the government is spending more money than it is earning. Budget deficits are typically covered by borrowing, which may include issuing government bonds or borrowing from international financial institutions. Persistent budget deficits can lead to an increase in national debt and have long-term economic implications.</p>
