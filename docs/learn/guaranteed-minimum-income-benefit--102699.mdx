# Guaranteed Minimum Income Benefit 

<ArticleMeta id={102699} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:59:02'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A guaranteed minimum income benefit (GMIB) is an optional rider that annuitants can purchase for their retirement annuities. When the&nbsp;annuity&nbsp;has been annuitized, this specific option guarantees that the&nbsp;annuitant&nbsp;will receive a minimum value of payments on a regular basis, regardless of other circumstances.</p>
