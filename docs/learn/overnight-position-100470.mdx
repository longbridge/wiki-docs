# Overnight Position

<ArticleMeta id={100470} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>An overnight position refers to any open position held by a financial institution or investor at the end of a trading day, which is carried over into the next trading day. These positions are exposed to overnight market risk. Overnight positions can be either long (buy) or short (sell) and can occur in various markets such as forex, stocks, and futures.</p>
