# Wide-Ranging Days

<ArticleMeta id={101191} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Wide-Ranging Days refer to days in the stock market where the price of a security experiences significant volatility, with a substantial difference between the highest and lowest prices. This situation typically reflects high market volatility.</p>
