# Forward Premium

<ArticleMeta id={101148} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Forward Premium refers to a situation in the foreign exchange market where the forward exchange rate of a currency is higher than its spot exchange rate. Specifically, when you enter into a forward contract to buy or sell a currency at a future date, if the forward rate is higher than the current spot rate, this situation is called a forward premium. A forward premium typically reflects the market's expectation that the currency will appreciate in the future, or it may be due to the interest rate differential between the two countries.</p>
