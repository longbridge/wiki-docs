# Lease Receivables

<ArticleMeta id={122} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:05:38'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Lease Receivables refer to the amounts of lease payments that a lessor is entitled to receive from a lessee under a finance lease agreement. These payments typically include both principal and interest components and are paid in installments over the lease term.</p>
