# Total Quality Management 

<ArticleMeta id={102084} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 18:33:12'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Total Quality Management (TQM) is a management approach aimed at improving the overall quality and performance of an organization through the involvement of all employees, continuous improvement, and a customer-centric focus. TQM emphasizes the responsibility of every employee in quality management, focusing on process optimization and continuous improvement to achieve long-term success and customer satisfaction.</p><p>Key characteristics include:</p><ol><li><strong>Employee Involvement</strong>: TQM stresses the participation of all employees, from top management to frontline workers, in the quality management process, with everyone being responsible for quality.</li><li><strong>Customer Focus</strong>: Centers on understanding and meeting customer needs and expectations to enhance customer satisfaction.</li><li><strong>Process Optimization</strong>: Focuses on optimizing every aspect of business processes to improve quality and efficiency.</li><li><strong>Continuous Improvement</strong>: Pursues excellence and best practices through ongoing improvement and innovation.</li><li><strong>Systematic Approach</strong>: Uses a systematic approach to integrate quality management into all organizational activities and processes, ensuring overall coordination and consistency.</li></ol><p>Core principles of Total Quality Management:</p><ol><li><strong>Customer-Centric</strong>: Understand and exceed customer needs and expectations.</li><li><strong>Employee Involvement</strong>: Engage all employees in quality management, working together to achieve organizational goals.</li><li><strong>Process Approach</strong>: View activities and resources as a process, managing and improving them systematically.</li><li><strong>System Management</strong>: Manage interrelated processes to achieve organizational objectives.</li><li><strong>Continuous Improvement</strong>: Continuously enhance overall organizational performance.</li><li><strong>Fact-Based Decision Making</strong>: Use data and information analysis to make informed decisions.</li><li><strong>Mutually Beneficial Supplier Relationships</strong>: Establish win-win relationships with suppliers to improve the overall quality of the supply chain.</li></ol>
