# War Bond

<ArticleMeta id={101177} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>War Bond refers to bonds issued by a government during wartime to raise funds for the war effort. These bonds are typically sold to the general public to encourage citizens to invest and support the nation's war efforts. War bonds usually have lower interest rates and may come with patriotic messaging to inspire citizens to purchase them.&nbsp;</p>
