# Euronext Paris

<ArticleMeta id={179} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:02:44'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Euronext Paris, also known as the Paris Stock Exchange, is the main stock exchange in France. It is the second largest stock exchange in Europe and one of the oldest stock exchanges in the world. Euronext Paris provides trading and clearing services for financial products such as stocks, bonds, and derivatives, and holds an important position in the international financial market.
