# Paper Trade

<ArticleMeta id={101205} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Paper trading is a simulated trading activity where investors execute buy and sell operations without using real money. It is typically used to test trading strategies, learn market operations, and improve trading skills. The goal of paper trading is to allow investors to practice in a real market environment without bearing actual financial risk.</p>
