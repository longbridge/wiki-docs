# Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect and Shenzhen-Hong Kong Stock Connect

<ArticleMeta id={177} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:03:01'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

The Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect is an interconnection mechanism between the two stock markets in Mainland China and Hong Kong. The Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect allows qualified investors registered in the Mainland market to invest in stocks listed in the Mainland through Hong Kong institutions, and also allows qualified investors registered in the Hong Kong market to invest in stocks listed in Hong Kong through Mainland institutions. This mechanism helps facilitate the interconnection and capital flow between the two markets.
