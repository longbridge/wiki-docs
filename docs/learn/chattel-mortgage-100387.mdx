# Chattel Mortgage

<ArticleMeta id={100387} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

A chattel mortgage is a loan to purchase movable personal property, such as a manufactured home or construction equipment. The property, or chattel, secures the loan, and the lender holds an ownership interest.Chattel loans are commonly referred to as security agreements. The terms “personal property security,” “lien on personal property,” or even “movable hypothecation” are other synonyms for a chattel mortgage.
