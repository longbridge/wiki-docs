# Days Sales Outstanding

<ArticleMeta id={125} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:05:31'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) measures the average number of days it takes for a company to collect payment after a sale has been made. It is an indicator of the efficiency of a company's accounts receivable management and the payment behavior of its customers.</p>

Shorter turnover days indicate that the company can collect its receivables faster, improving cash flow efficiency.</p><p><strong>Origin:</strong> The concept of Accounts Receivable Turnover Days originated from liquidity analysis in financial management.
