# Surcharge

<ArticleMeta id={100088} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A surcharge is an additional charge, fee, or tax that is added to the cost of a good or service beyond the initially quoted price. A surcharge is often added to an existing tax and is not included in the stated price of the good or service.&nbsp;</p><p>The amount of a surcharge varies and can be a fixed amount or a percentage of This charge may be imposed because of a governing body's need for additional revenue or to defray the cost of increased commodity pricing.</p>
