# Leveraged Recapitalization

<ArticleMeta id={100461} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Leveraged recapitalization is a corporate financial strategy that involves restructuring a company's capital structure by increasing its debt. Typically, a company raises funds by issuing new debt and uses these funds to repurchase shares, pay special dividends, or distribute other forms of shareholder returns. The goal is to alter the company's capital structure by increasing the proportion of debt relative to equity.</p>
