# Insurance Reserves

<ArticleMeta id={25} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:10:40'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Insurance contract reserve is the fund reserve set up by insurance companies to fulfill their obligations under insurance contracts. The main purpose of insurance contract reserves is to ensure that they have sufficient funds to pay insurance claims and fulfill their obligations to policyholders. Insurance companies need to set up and accrue insurance contract reserves in accordance with laws and regulations and the provisions of insurance contracts.
