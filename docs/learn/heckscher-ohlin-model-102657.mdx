# Heckscher-Ohlin Model

<ArticleMeta id={102657} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 14:57:45'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Heckscher-Ohlin model is an economic theory proposing that countries export what they can most efficiently and plentifully produce. It's also referred to as the H-O model or 2x2x2 model. It's used to evaluate trade and the equilibrium of trade between two countries that have varying specialties and natural resources.</p><p>The model emphasizes the export of goods that require factors of production a country has in abundance. It also emphasizes the import of goods that a nation&nbsp;can't produce as efficiently. It takes the position that countries should ideally export materials and resources that they have an excess of while proportionately importing those resources they need.</p>
