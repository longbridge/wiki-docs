# Free Cash Flow to Equity

<ArticleMeta id={227} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:38'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Free Cash Flow to Equity (FCFE) is the cash flow available to a company's equity shareholders after accounting for all operating expenses, capital expenditures, and debt repayments. It represents the amount of cash that can be distributed to shareholders after fulfilling all obligations. FCFE is a critical metric for assessing shareholder returns and the value delivered to equity holders.</p>
