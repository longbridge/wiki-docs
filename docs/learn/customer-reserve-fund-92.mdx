# Customer Reserve Fund

<ArticleMeta id={92} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:07:16'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The customer reserve fund, also known as customer deposits, refers to the funds held by financial institutions (such as banks, securities companies) or payment service providers (such as payment platforms) on behalf of their customers. These funds are typically pre-deposited by customers for future payment or transaction needs. The management and usage of customer reserve funds are strictly regulated to ensure the safety of the funds and the protection of customer rights.</p>
