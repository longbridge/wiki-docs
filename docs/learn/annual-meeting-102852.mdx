# Annual General Meeting

<ArticleMeta id={102852} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 02:20:40'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Annual General Meeting (AGM) is a yearly gathering of a publicly traded company's shareholders, designed to provide shareholders with an overview of the company's annual performance, vote on the election of board members, approve financial statements, and address other significant matters. The AGM is a crucial avenue for shareholders to participate in corporate governance and decision-making. The primary agenda typically includes reviewing the company's financial reports, electing board members, selecting auditors, and discussing dividend policies, among other important issues.</p>
