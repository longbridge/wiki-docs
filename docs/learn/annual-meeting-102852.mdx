# Annual Meeting

<ArticleMeta id={102852} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 02:20:40'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

The annual general meeting of shareholders is a meeting of shareholders held by a listed company every year, with main agendas including the review of the company's financial reports, election of members of the board of directors, selection of auditing firms, dividend policies, etc.
