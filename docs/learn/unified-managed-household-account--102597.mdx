# Unified Managed Household Account 

<ArticleMeta id={102597} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:59:02'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A unified managed household account (UMHA) is a privately managed account that consolidates multiple unaffiliated products including mutual funds, ETFs, and individual securities. This type of account allows immediate family members, such as parents and children, to access the account.</p><p>A unified managed household account allows for ease of administration for the financial institution, along with greater transparency for the investing family.</p>
