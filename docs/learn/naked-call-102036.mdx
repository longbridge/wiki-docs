# Naked Call

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>A Naked Call is an options trading strategy where an investor sells call options without owning the underlying security. This strategy carries a higher level of risk as the investor may be required to purchase the underlying security at market prices to fulfill the option contract's obligations. It contrasts with a covered call strategy where the investor holds the underlying security.</span></p>
