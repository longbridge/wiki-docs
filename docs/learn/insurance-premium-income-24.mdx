# Insurance Premium Income

<ArticleMeta id={24} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:09:39'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Insurance business income refers to the income obtained by insurance companies through the sale of insurance products. Insurance business income includes premium income, investment income, and dividend income, etc.
