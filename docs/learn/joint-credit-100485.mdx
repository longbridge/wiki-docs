# Joint Credit

<ArticleMeta id={100485} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Joint Credit refers to a credit line or loan applied for and used by two or more individuals or entities together. Joint credit is often used for purchasing large assets such as homes or cars, or for jointly operated businesses. The parties involved share everything about the debt including the credit limit and the responsibility to repay it back to the lender.&nbsp;</p>
