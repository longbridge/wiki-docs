# Legal Rate Of Interest

<ArticleMeta id={100413} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Legal Rate of Interest is the interest rate established by law or regulation, typically used to calculate interest on debts or compensation amounts when no explicit interest rate is agreed upon. For instance, if a loan agreement does not specify an interest rate, a court may use the legal rate of interest to determine the interest payable. The legal rate of interest is often set to protect borrowers from exorbitant interest rates while ensuring that lenders receive a fair return. The legal rate of interest can vary between different countries or regions and may be adjusted based on economic conditions or policy changes.</p>
