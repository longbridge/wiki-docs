# Cash And Cash Equivalents

<ArticleMeta id={102606} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 15:48:16'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cash and cash equivalents are a line item on the balance sheet that reports the value of a company's assets that are cash or can be converted into cash immediately. Cash equivalents include bank accounts and some types of marketable securities, such as debt securities with maturities of less than 90 days. However, cash equivalents often do not include equity or stock holdings because they can fluctuate in value.</p>
