# Research and Development Expenses

<ArticleMeta id={198} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:01:50'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Research and development expenses are the costs incurred by a company for researching and developing new products, new technologies, or improving existing products and technologies. Research and development expenses include employee salaries, research equipment, experimental materials, patent application fees, etc.
