# Compensatory Damages

<ArticleMeta id={100344} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Compensatory damages are monetary awards granted by a court in a legal proceeding to compensate a party for actual losses suffered due to breach of contract, tort, or other wrongful acts. The purpose of compensatory damages is to restore the injured party to the financial position they would have been in if the harm had not occurred. These damages can cover a range of losses, including medical expenses, property damage, lost wages, and emotional distress.</p>
