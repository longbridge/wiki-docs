# Dividend Yield

<ArticleMeta id={100093} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

The dividend yield, expressed as a percentage, is a financial ratio (dividend/price) that shows how much a company pays out in dividends each year relative to its stock price.The reciprocal of the dividend yield is the total dividends paid/net income which is the dividend payout ratio.
