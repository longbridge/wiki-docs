# Dividend Yield

<ArticleMeta id={100093} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(17,17,17)"}}>The dividend yield is a financial ratio that shows how much a company pays out in dividends each year relative to its stock price. The reciprocal of the dividend yield is the total dividends paid/net income which is the dividend payout ratio.</span></p>
