# Cost Basis

<ArticleMeta id={100308} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cost basis refers to the original value of an asset for tax purposes, used to determine capital gains or losses when the asset is sold. It typically includes the original purchase price of the asset and can be adjusted for various associated costs such as commissions, taxes, and improvement expenses. Cost basis is crucial for determining the taxable amount when an asset is sold, as capital gains or losses are calculated by subtracting the cost basis from the selling price.</p>
