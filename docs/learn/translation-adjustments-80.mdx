# Translation Adjustments

<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Translation adjustments refer to the differences that arise when a company's foreign currency financial statements are translated into the reporting currency of the parent company due to changes in exchange rates. These adjustments reflect the impact of exchange rate fluctuations on the financial statements.</p>
