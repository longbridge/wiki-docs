# Fair Market Value 

<ArticleMeta id={102322} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 16:43:01'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Fair Market Value (FMV) refers to the price at which an asset would change hands between a willing buyer and a willing seller in an arm's-length transaction, neither being under any compulsion to buy or sell and both having reasonable knowledge of the relevant facts. FMV is a crucial concept in asset pricing and valuation, commonly used in taxation, accounting, legal, and financial contexts. Determining FMV typically involves considering market supply and demand, the specific characteristics of the asset, transaction conditions, and relevant market information. FMV provides a fair and objective basis for asset valuation for investors, tax authorities, appraisers, and others.</p>
