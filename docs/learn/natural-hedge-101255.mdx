# Natural Hedge

<ArticleMeta id={101255} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A natural hedge refers to a strategy that reduces financial risks in the normal operation of an institution. It is typically done by investing in different assets and financial instruments with negative correlations among them. The conventional financial hedging strategy usually contains derivatives and forwards. The natural hedging strategy does not require sophisticated financial instruments.</p>
