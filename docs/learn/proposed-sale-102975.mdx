# Proposed Sale

<ArticleMeta id={102975} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 14:20:30'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Proposed sale refers to the intention or proposal of a company or individual to sell a certain asset or property. Proposed sales usually require appropriate procedures and negotiations to determine the terms, price, and other related matters of the sale.
