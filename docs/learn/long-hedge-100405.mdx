# Long Hedge

<ArticleMeta id={100405} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A long hedge is a risk management strategy where an investor buys futures contracts to hedge against the risk of future price increases in the spot market. This strategy is typically used to protect against the risk of rising prices for assets or commodities that the investor plans to purchase in the future.</p>
