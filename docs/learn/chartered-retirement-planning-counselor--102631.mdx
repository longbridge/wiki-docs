# Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor 

<ArticleMeta id={102631} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 00:25:50'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Chartered retirement planning counselor (CRPC) is a professional designation awarded by the College for Financial Planning to candidates who successfully complete its study program and pass a final examination.</p><p>A CRPC designation is seen as a boost for a financial planner's job opportunities, professional reputation, and pay prospects.</p><p>Successful applicants earn the right to use the CRPC designation with their names for two years. Every two years, CRPC professionals must complete 16 hours of continuing education and pay a small fee to continue using the designation.</p>
