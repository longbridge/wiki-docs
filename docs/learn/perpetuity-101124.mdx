# Perpetuity

<ArticleMeta id={101124} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Perpetuity is a financial instrument that refers to a series of cash flows that continue indefinitely. This means that the payments will go on forever, rather than ending after a specific period. Perpetuity is often used to evaluate certain types of investments or financial arrangements, such as pension plans or endowment funds.</p>
