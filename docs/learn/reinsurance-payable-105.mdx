# Reinsurance payable

<ArticleMeta id={105} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:06:28'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Reinsurance payable refers to the amounts that an insurance company is obligated to pay to a reinsurance company under reinsurance contracts. When an insurance company transfers a portion of its risk to another reinsurance company, it needs to pay a certain premium or a share of the claims to the reinsurer.</p>
