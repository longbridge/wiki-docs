# Continuing operating net profit

<ArticleMeta id={156} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:03:44'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Continuing operating net profit is the net profit obtained by a company in normal operating activities. It reflects the net profit created by a company under continuing operations. Continuing operating net profit is typically used to evaluate a company's profitability and sustainability.
