# Main Business Profit

<ArticleMeta id={10} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:11:12'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Main business profit refers to the profit obtained by a company through its main business. The main business profit reflects the core profitability of the company and can be used to evaluate the company's operating condition and profitability.<br/><br/>The formula for calculating main business profit is:&nbsp;<br/>Main Business Profit = Main Business Revenue − Main Business Cost − Main Business Taxes and Surcharges Main Business Profit=Main Business Revenue−Main Business Cost−Main Business Taxes and Surcharges</p>
