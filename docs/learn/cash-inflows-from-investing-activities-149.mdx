# Cash Inflows from Investing Activities

<ArticleMeta id={149} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:04:03'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Cash inflows from investment activities refer to the cash received by a company through investment activities. Investment activities include the purchase or sale of long-term assets, investments in other companies, etc.
