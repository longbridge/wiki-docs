# Analysis Of Variance 

<ArticleMeta id={100418} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) is a statistical method used to compare the means of three or more samples to determine if they come from populations with the same mean. ANOVA assesses the variability within groups and between groups by comparing the variance among group means to the variance within the groups. If the between-group variance is significantly larger than the within-group variance, it can be inferred that there are significant differences among the sample means. ANOVA is widely used in experimental design, agriculture, psychology, social sciences, and medical research.</p>
