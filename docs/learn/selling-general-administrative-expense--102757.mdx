# Selling, General, and Administrative Expense 

<ArticleMeta id={102757} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:28:43'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Selling, general, and administrative expenses (SG&amp;A) include all non-production expenses for a reporting period. Examples of these expenses are marketing, advertising, rent, and utilities.</p><p>This line item includes nearly all business costs that aren't directly attributable to making a product or performing a service. SG&amp;A consists of the costs of managing a company and the expenses of delivering its products or services.</p>
