# Receipt in Advance

<ArticleMeta id={291} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:56:32'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Receipts in advance refers to the funds that a company receives in advance from customers when selling goods or providing services. Receipts in advance are considered liabilities for a company because the company must fulfill the related transactions when delivering goods or providing services in the future. Receipts in advance typically refer to funds that will be refunded to customers within the next year or within one operating cycle exceeding one year.</p>
