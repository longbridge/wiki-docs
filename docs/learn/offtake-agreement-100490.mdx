# Purchase and Sale Agreement

<ArticleMeta id={100490} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Purchase and Sale Agreement (PSA) is a formal contract between a buyer and a seller regarding the purchase and sale of goods or services. The agreement details the terms and conditions of the transaction, including price, quantity, delivery time, payment methods, quality standards, and more. The PSA plays a crucial role in commercial transactions by clearly defining the rights and obligations of both parties, thereby reducing disputes and misunderstandings. Key components typically include the contracting parties, the subject matter of the contract, price and payment terms, delivery terms, quality standards and inspection, breach of contract liability, force majeure, dispute resolution, and the contract's effectiveness and termination.</p>
