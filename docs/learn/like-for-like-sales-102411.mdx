# Like-for-Like Sales

<ArticleMeta id={102411} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Like-for-like sales are used as an adjusted growth metric that includes revenues generated from stores or products with similar characteristics while omitting any with distinct differences that could skew the numbers.Like-for-like sales are also referred to as comparable-store sales, comps, same-store sales, or identical-store sales.
