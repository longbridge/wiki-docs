# Hedge Fund

<ArticleMeta id={100258} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A hedge fund is a private investment fund that pools capital from wealthy individuals, institutional investors, or other accredited investors. Hedge funds employ a variety of investment strategies, such as leverage, short selling, and derivatives, to achieve high returns and manage risk. Hedge fund managers typically have significant flexibility in their investment choices, allowing them to invest in a wide range of asset classes globally, including stocks, bonds, currencies, and commodities. Due to their complex strategies and higher risk profiles, hedge funds often require investors to have a high tolerance for risk and a longer investment horizon. The fee structure of hedge funds usually includes a management fee and a performance fee.</p>
