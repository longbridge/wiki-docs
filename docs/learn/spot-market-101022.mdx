# Spot Market

<ArticleMeta id={101022} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A spot market is a market where buyers and sellers immediately conduct the delivery and payment of goods, currencies, or other assets at the time of the transaction. In this market, the transaction price is determined instantly, and the delivery and payment are usually completed shortly after the transaction is agreed upon.</p>
