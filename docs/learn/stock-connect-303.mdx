# Stock Connect

<ArticleMeta id={303} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:56:06'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Stock Connect refers to the mechanism under the Shanghai-Hong Kong Stock Connect and Shenzhen-Hong Kong Stock Connect, which allows overseas investors to buy and sell stocks listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange and Shenzhen Stock Exchange through the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. The introduction of Stock Connect provides overseas investors with a more convenient channel to invest in the mainland Chinese stock markets.</p>
