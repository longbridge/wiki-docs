# Cash Outflows from Operating Activities

<ArticleMeta id={210} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:01:10'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Operating cash outflow refers to the total amount of cash outflow that a company pays in its operating activities. This includes the purchase of goods, cash payments received for services rendered, payment of taxes, payment of wages and benefits, payment of other cash related to operating activities, etc.
