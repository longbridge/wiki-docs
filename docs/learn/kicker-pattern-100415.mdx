# Kicker Pattern

<ArticleMeta id={100415} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:34:48'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Kicker Pattern is a powerful signal in technical analysis used to identify strong market trend reversals. It typically appears in stock, futures, or forex markets. The Kicker Pattern consists of two candlesticks. The first candlestick is usually a long-bodied candle in the direction of the current trend. The second candlestick is a long-bodied candle in the opposite direction of the first candlestick. There is a significant gap between the opening price of the second candlestick and the closing price of the first candlestick (i.e., the opening price is either higher or lower than the closing price of the previous candle), indicating a substantial shift in market sentiment. The Kicker Pattern is considered a strong signal that the market is about to undergo a significant reversal.</p>
