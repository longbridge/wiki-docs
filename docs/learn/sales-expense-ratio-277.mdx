# Sales Expense Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={277} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:57:35'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The sales expense ratio measures the proportion of sales expenses to total sales revenue over a specific period. Sales expenses include advertising costs, sales staff salaries, sales commissions, and promotional expenses. The sales expense ratio is a key indicator of a company's sales efficiency and cost control capabilities. A higher ratio may indicate significant investment in sales and marketing activities, while a lower ratio suggests effective control over sales expenses.</p>
