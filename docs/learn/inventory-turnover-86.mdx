# Inventory Turnover

<p>Inventory Turnover is an indicator that measures the efficiency of inventory operations in a company, indicating the average time it takes for inventory to be purchased, sold, and cash collected. It can help companies evaluate the effectiveness of inventory management and the efficiency of capital utilization.</p>
