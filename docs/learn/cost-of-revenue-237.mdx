# Cost of Revenue

<ArticleMeta id={237} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:01'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cost of Revenue refers to the cost incurred by a company during a certain accounting period through the sale of goods or the provision of services. It includes the cost of direct materials, direct labor, and manufacturing costs directly related to the production of goods or the provision of services. Operating cost is an important component in calculating gross profit and net profit of a company, and it has important significance in cost control and business decision-making.</p>
