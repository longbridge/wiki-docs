# Underbanked

<ArticleMeta id={102144} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Underbanked refers to individuals who have a bank account but do not fully utilize traditional financial services. These individuals have basic bank accounts but rely heavily on alternative financial services such as check cashing, prepaid debit cards, money orders, and payday loans due to various reasons like trust issues, high costs, or lack of financial literacy.</p><p>Key characteristics include:</p><p>Having Bank Accounts: Underbanked individuals typically have one or more bank accounts.<br/>Underutilization: Despite having bank accounts, they seldom or never use services such as savings, loans, and credit cards offered by banks.<br/>Alternative Financial Services: Frequently use non-traditional financial services like check cashing, prepaid debit cards, money orders, and payday loans.<br/>Financial Exclusion: Face financial exclusion or difficulty accessing traditional financial services, leading to reliance on costly and high-risk alternative financial services.<br/>Example of Underbanked application:<br/>Suppose a person has a bank account but prefers to cash their paycheck at a check cashing company due to mistrust in banks or high banking fees. They also use a prepaid debit card for daily transactions. This individual is not fully utilizing the deposit and loan services offered by their bank and is considered underbanked.</p>
