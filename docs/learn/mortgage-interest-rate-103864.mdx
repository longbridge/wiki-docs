# Mortgage Interest Rate

<ArticleMeta id={103864} updatedAt={'2023-10-13 08:20:17'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The mortgage interest rate is the interest rate charged by a financial institution on a loan taken out to purchase real estate. Mortgage interest rates can be fixed or variable, and the specific rate is influenced by market interest rates, the borrower's creditworthiness, and the loan term. The mortgage interest rate directly affects the borrower's repayment amount and the cost of purchasing a home, making it a crucial reference for homebuyers and the real estate market.</p>
