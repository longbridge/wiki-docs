# White Candlestick

<ArticleMeta id={101287} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A white candlestick depicts a period where the security's price has closed at a higher level than where it had opened. It is a point on a security's candlestick chart representing a bullish period.</p><p>On some charts, an up-candlestick may be depicted as either green or black. These may be contrasted with a red candlestick, which denotes a lower closing price than the prior period.</p>
