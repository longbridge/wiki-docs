# Keltner Channel

<ArticleMeta id={102000} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:58'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>Keltner Channels are a technical analysis tool that helps traders determine market trends by plotting volatility-based bands on either side of an asset's price. These channels use the average true range (ATR) to measure volatility, generating upper and lower bands. Breakouts above or below these bands may indicate the continuation or change in trend.</span></p>
