# Selling, General and Administrative Expenses

<ArticleMeta id={274} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:57:44'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Sales and administrative expenses are the sum of various expenses incurred by a company during the process of selling products or providing services. These expenses include sales expenses, advertising expenses, promotional expenses, management salaries, office expenses, etc. Sales and administrative expenses are necessary expenditures in a company's business activities and have a certain impact on the company's profitability and operating efficiency.
