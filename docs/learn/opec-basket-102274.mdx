# OPEC Basket

<ArticleMeta id={102274} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 17:01:55'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The OPEC Basket, or OPEC Basket Price, is a significant benchmark used by the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) to gauge international oil prices. This price is a weighted average of various crude oil blends produced by OPEC member countries. The OPEC Basket reflects the market price of the primary crude types produced by OPEC members, providing a comprehensive and authoritative measure. The basket price is updated daily and is widely used to analyze global oil market trends and to formulate OPEC's production policies.</p>
