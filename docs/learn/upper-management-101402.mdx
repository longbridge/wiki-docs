# Upper Management

<ArticleMeta id={101402} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Upper Management refers to the senior executives in a company or organization who are responsible for making strategic decisions and setting the overall direction. These individuals typically include the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Chief Operating Officer (COO), Chief Financial Officer (CFO), among others. Their primary responsibilities include setting company goals, formulating long-term strategies, allocating resources, managing the executive team, and ensuring the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the company's operations. Upper management plays a crucial role in the success and growth of a company, with their decisions directly impacting the company's direction and performance.</p>
