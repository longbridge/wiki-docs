# Noncurrent Assets

<ArticleMeta id={101379} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Noncurrent Assets refer to assets that a company does not expect to convert into cash within one accounting year (or one normal operating cycle). These assets are typically used for long-term investment or operational purposes and have a longer useful life.</p>
