# Environmental, Social, and Governance investing

<ArticleMeta id={102719} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:40:49'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>ESG stands for&nbsp;environmental, social, and governance.&nbsp;ESG investing refers to how companies score on these responsibility metrics and standards for potential investments. Environmental criteria gauge how a company safeguards the environment. Social criteria examine&nbsp;how it manages relationships with employees, suppliers, customers, and communities. Governance measures a company’s leadership, executive pay, audits,&nbsp;internal controls,&nbsp;and shareholder rights.</p>
