# dividend yield

<ArticleMeta id={103020} updatedAt={'2023-09-29 02:20:23'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Dividend yield refers to the ratio of the dividends distributed per share by a company to the current price of the stock. Dividend yield is one of the indicators used to evaluate the return on investment in stocks, and a higher dividend yield usually implies a higher investment return.
