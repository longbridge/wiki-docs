# dividend declaration

<ArticleMeta id={103065} updatedAt={'2023-09-29 08:20:55'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Dividend declaration refers to the act of a company announcing the payment of dividends to shareholders. Dividends are a portion of the company's profits distributed to shareholders on a per share basis. Dividend declarations typically affect stock prices and investors' perception of the company.
