# Percentage Change

<ArticleMeta id={101288} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Percentage change is used for many purposes in finance, often to represent the price change of a stock over time, expressed as a percentage. The formula used to calculate this change as a percentage is a simple mathematical concept that varies slightly depending on whether the change is an increase or a decrease.Read on to learn how to calculate percentage change.
