# Mortgage Originator

A mortgage originator is an institution or individual that works with a borrower to complete a a home loan transaction. A mortgage originatorÂ is the original mortgage lender and can be either a mortgage broker or a mortgage banker. Mortgage originators are part of the primary mortgage market and must work with underwriters and loan processors from the application date until closing to gather the necessary documentation and guide the file through the approval process.
