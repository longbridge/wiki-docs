# Yield Tilt Index Fund

<ArticleMeta id={102591} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:02'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

A yield tilt index fund is a type of fund that invests in stocks or securities that mirrors the holdings of a market index but contains a higher weighting towards higher-yielding investments. A yield tilt index fund can be a mutual fund, which is a basket of securities that are actively managed by a portfolio or fund manager.A yield tilt index fund can also be an exchange-traded fund (ETF), which merely mirrors an index of stocks.
