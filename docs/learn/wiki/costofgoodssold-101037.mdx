# Cost of Goods Sold

<ArticleMeta id={101037} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Cost of goods sold (COGS) refers to the direct costs of producing the goods sold by a company. This amount includes the cost of the materials and labor directly used to create the good. It excludes indirect expenses, such as distribution costs and sales force costs.Cost of goods sold is also referred to as "cost of sales."</p>
