# Net Debt

<ArticleMeta id={100124} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Net debt is a liquidity metric used to determine how well a company can pay all of its debts if they were due immediately. Net debt shows how much debt a company has on its balance sheet compared to its liquid assets.Net debt shows how much cash would remain if all debts were paid off and if a company has enough liquidity to meet its debt obligations.
