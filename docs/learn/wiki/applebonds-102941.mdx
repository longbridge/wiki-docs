# Apple bonds

<ArticleMeta id={102941} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 08:20:34'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Apple's bonds are debt securities issued by Apple. Bonds are a form of borrowing, and bondholders can receive fixed interest income and receive the principal back when the bond matures. The purpose of Apple issuing bonds is to raise funds to support the company's business expansion and investment plans. Apple's bonds usually have high credit ratings and are widely watched by investors.
