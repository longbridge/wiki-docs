# GTV

<ArticleMeta id={102806} updatedAt={'2023-09-27 16:21:37'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Gross Transaction Value (GTV) generally refers to the total transaction amount on a platform. GTV does not deduct items such as returns that reduce income, and is the aggregate trade value at original price.</p><p>Generally, intermediary platforms such as Meituan and Beike use GTV as an evaluation metric.</p>
