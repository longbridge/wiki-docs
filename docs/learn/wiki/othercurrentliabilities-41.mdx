# Other current liabilities

<ArticleMeta id={41} updatedAt={'2023-09-13 19:03:51'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Other current liabilities refer to other debts that a company needs to repay within one year or within one operating cycle exceeding one year, excluding short-term borrowings, accounts payable, bills payable, wages payable, welfare expenses payable, income taxes payable, dividends payable, interest payable, advances from customers, accrued expenses, other payables, and other taxes payable.</p>
