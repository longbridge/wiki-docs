# FANG Stocks

<ArticleMeta id={102272} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

In finance, the acronym "FANG" refers to the stocks of four prominent American technology companies: Meta (META) (formerly Facebook), Amazon (AMZN), Netflix (NFLX), and Alphabet/Google (GOOG). FANG stocks are famous for the impressive growth they have experienced, with each member's stock price more than doubling at times since 2018.In 2017, the company Apple (AAPL) was also added by some analysts, resulting in the new acronym "FAANG." Apple's stock price increased more than 113% between August 2018 and August 2023.
