# Gross Margin

<ArticleMeta id={102851} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 02:20:26'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Gross profit margin refers to the ratio of the profit remaining after deducting direct costs from sales revenue to sales revenue. Direct costs include material costs, labor costs, and manufacturing expenses required for the production or sale of products. Gross profit margin can reflect a company's profitability and cost management level.
