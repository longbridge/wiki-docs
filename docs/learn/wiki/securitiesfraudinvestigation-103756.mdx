# securities fraud investigation

<ArticleMeta id={103756} updatedAt={'2023-10-12 00:20:18'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Securities fraud investigation refers to the investigation of suspected fraudulent activities in the securities market. Securities fraud refers to the behavior of companies, institutions, or individuals deceiving investors in the securities market through false statements, omission of important information, and other means in order to obtain illegal benefits.
