# Earned Income Credit 

<ArticleMeta id={102468} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:00'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Earnings before tax (EBT) is a measure of financial performance. It reveals a company's earnings before taxes are deducted, is calculated by subtracting all expenses excluding taxes from revenue, and appears as a line item in the income statement.EBT is sometimes also called pre-tax income, profit before tax, or income before income taxes.
