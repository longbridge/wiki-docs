# interim results

<ArticleMeta id={102853} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 02:20:40'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Mid-term performance refers to the performance achieved by a listed company within the first 6 months or 9 months of a year, typically presented through the release of semi-annual reports or third-quarter reports. Mid-term performance reflects the company's operating condition and prospects in the current economic environment.
