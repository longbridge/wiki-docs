# Gross Profit

<ArticleMeta id={297} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:56:22'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Gross profit refers to the difference between the sales revenue and the direct costs remaining after a company sells products or provides services. Direct costs include costs directly related to sales, such as raw material costs, labor costs, and manufacturing costs. Gross profit is the actual profit obtained by a company in its business activities and can be used to evaluate the company's profitability and operational efficiency.
