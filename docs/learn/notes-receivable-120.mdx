# Notes Receivable

<ArticleMeta id={120} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:05:41'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Notes receivable refers to the promissory notes issued by other entities that a company or individual holds and promises to pay a certain amount to the holder within a certain period of time. These notes can be considered as receivables for the company or individual, and can be cashed or transferred to other entities or individuals when the notes are due.
