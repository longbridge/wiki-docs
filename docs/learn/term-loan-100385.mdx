# Term Loan

<ArticleMeta id={100385} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Term Loan is a type of loan where the borrower receives a lump sum of money from a lending institution and agrees to repay the principal along with interest over a set period. Term loans are typically used for purchasing fixed assets, expanding business operations, or making significant investments. Repayment of term loans is typically structured in installments, where the borrower makes monthly, quarterly, or annual payments of principal and interest.</p>
