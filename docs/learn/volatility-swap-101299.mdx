# Volatility Swap

<ArticleMeta id={101299} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Volatility Swap is a financial derivative that allows investors to trade the future volatility of an underlying asset. In this contract, the parties agree to exchange cash flows at a future date based on the difference between the realized volatility of the underlying asset and the agreed-upon volatility. The buyer of a volatility swap typically benefits if the realized volatility of the underlying asset is higher than the agreed-upon volatility, while the seller benefits if the realized volatility is lower. Volatility swaps are widely used for hedging and speculation, particularly in markets with uncertain volatility.</p>
