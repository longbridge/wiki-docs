# Mortgage Bond

<ArticleMeta id={100373} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Mortgage Bond is a type of bond that is secured by physical assets, typically real estate, which serve as collateral. The issuing company or institution pledges its assets to secure the bond, thereby reducing the risk for investors. If the issuer fails to make timely payments of principal and interest on the bond, the bondholders have the right to liquidate the collateralized assets to recover their investment. Due to the security provided by the physical assets, mortgage bonds are considered relatively safe investments and generally offer lower interest rates. Mortgage bonds are commonly used to finance real estate and infrastructure projects.</p>
