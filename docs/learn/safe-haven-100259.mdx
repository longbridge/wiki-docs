# Safe Haven

<ArticleMeta id={100259} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A safe haven is an investment that is expected to retain or increase its value during times of market turbulence or economic uncertainty. Investors turn to safe havens to protect their capital from losses during periods of financial instability, economic downturns, or geopolitical risks. Common examples of safe haven assets include gold, the Swiss franc, U.S. Treasury bonds, and certain blue-chip stocks. The primary characteristic of a safe haven is its ability to maintain stability or appreciate in value when uncertainty in the market rises, providing a secure investment option for investors.</p>
