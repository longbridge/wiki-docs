# Straddle

<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgba(0,0,0,0.05)",color:"rgb(36,41,47)"}}>A straddle is an options trading strategy where the investor simultaneously buys an equal number of call and put options with the same strike price and expiration date. This strategy is used when the investor expects a significant price movement in the underlying asset but is uncertain about the direction. By employing a straddle, the investor can profit from substantial price movements in either direction. However, if the underlying asset's price remains relatively stable, the strategy may result in a loss.</span></p>
