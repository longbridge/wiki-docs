# Budget Variance

<ArticleMeta id={101138} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Budget variance refers to the difference between actual spending or revenue and the budgeted forecast. It can be a positive variance (where actual results are better than the budget) or a negative variance (where actual results fall short of the budget).&nbsp;</p>
