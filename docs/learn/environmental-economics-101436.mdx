# Environmental Economics

<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Environmental economics is the study of the cost-effective allocation, use, and protection of the world's natural resources.Economics, broadly speaking, is the study of how humans produce and consume goods and services. Environmental economics focuses on how they use and manage finite resources in a manner that serves the population while meeting concerns about environmental impact.This helps governments weigh the pros and cons of alternative measures and design appropriate environmental policies.
