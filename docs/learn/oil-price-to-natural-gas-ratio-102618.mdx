# Oil Price To Natural Gas Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={102618} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:59:02'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>As its name suggests, the oil price to natural gas ratio is a ratio in which the price of oil is the numerator and the price of natural gas is the denominator.&nbsp;</p><p>The purpose of the oil price to natural gas ratio is to capture the relative valuation of these two important energy commodities. It is widely used by commodities traders, energy analysts, and investors.</p>
