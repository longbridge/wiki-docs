# Guppy Multiple Moving Average 

<ArticleMeta id={102713} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 14:42:32'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Guppy Multiple Moving Average (GMMA) is a technical&nbsp;indicator that&nbsp;aims to anticipate a potential breakout in the price of an asset. The term gets its name from Daryl Guppy, an Australian financial columnist and book author who developed the concept in his book, "Trading Tactics."</p><p>The GMMA uses the exponential moving average (EMA) to capture the difference between price and value in a stock. A convergence in these factors is associated with a significant trend change. Guppy maintains that the GMMA is not a lagging indicator but a prior warning of a developing change in price and value.</p>
