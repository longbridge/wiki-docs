# Operating Earnings

<ArticleMeta id={101122} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Operating Earnings refer to the profit a company earns from its core business activities after deducting operating costs and expenses. It excludes non-operating income and expenses such as interest income, interest expenses, and taxes. Operating Earnings are an important metric for assessing a company's profitability from its primary business operations, as they reflect the company's performance in its regular business activities without being influenced by financial and investment activities.</p>
