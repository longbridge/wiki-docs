# January Effect

<ArticleMeta id={101085} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>January Effect refers to the phenomenon where stock prices tend to rise in January. This is typically attributed to investors selling stocks at the end of the year for tax benefits and then repurchasing them at the beginning of the new year.</p>
