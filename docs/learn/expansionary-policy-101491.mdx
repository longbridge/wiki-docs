# Expansionary Policy

<ArticleMeta id={101491} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Expansionary policy is a form of macroeconomic policy that seeks to encourage economic growth by increasing aggregate demand. It can consist of either monetary policy or fiscal policy, or a combination of the two. It is part of the general policy prescription of Keynesian economics to be used during economic slowdowns and recessions in order to moderate the downside of economic cycles. Expansionary policy is also known as loose policy.</p>
