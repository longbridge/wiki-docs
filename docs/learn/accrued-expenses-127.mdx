# Accrued Expenses

<ArticleMeta id={127} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:05:17'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Accrued expenses refer to expenses that have been incurred but have not yet been paid in cash in accounting. It is an accounting method of booking expenses to be paid in advance in order to correctly reflect the financial position and operating results of the enterprise.
