# Debt to Asset Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={261} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:58:19'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Debt to Asset Ratio is a key financial metric that measures the level of a company's debt relative to its total assets. It is calculated as: Debt to Asset Ratio = Total Debt / Total Assets. A higher ratio indicates greater reliance on debt financing and, consequently, higher financial risk.</p>
