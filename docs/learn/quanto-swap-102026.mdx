# Quanto Swap

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>A Quanto Swap is a cash-settled cross-currency interest rate swap involving the exchange of interest rates in two different currencies. In this swap, one party pays a floating or fixed interest rate in a foreign currency based on the notional principal (denominated in domestic currency), while the other party pays an equivalent domestic currency interest rate. Quanto swaps typically embed a fixed exchange rate, hence they are also known as guaranteed exchange rate swaps.</span></p>
