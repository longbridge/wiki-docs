# Large Cap 

<ArticleMeta id={100340} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Large Cap refers to the stocks of companies with a large market capitalization, typically in the billions of dollars. These companies are often well-established, financially stable, and have a significant presence in their respective industries. Large-cap stocks are generally considered to be less risky and more stable compared to smaller companies, making them suitable for long-term investments. Due to their size and influence, large-cap stocks usually have high liquidity and tend to perform relatively well during economic fluctuations.</p>
