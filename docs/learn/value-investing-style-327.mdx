# Value Investing Style

<ArticleMeta id={327} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:12'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Value investing style is an investment strategy where investors seek to invest in stocks that are trading below their intrinsic value. Value investors typically focus on financial metrics such as price-to-earnings (P/E) and price-to-book (P/B) ratios to identify undervalued stocks and profit when the market corrects their prices. The value investing style emphasizes a margin of safety and long-term holding, making it suitable for investors with lower risk tolerance who prioritize stable returns.</p>
