By Market Type: NIM can be classified into domestic market NIM and international market NIM. Domestic market NIM is mainly influenced by domestic economic conditions and policies, while international market NIM is affected by global economic situations and international lending rate fluctuations.</p><p>2. By Asset Type: This includes retail banking NIM and commercial banking NIM.
