# Supplemental Executive Retirement Plan

<ArticleMeta id={102644} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 15:21:42'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A supplemental executive retirement plan&nbsp;(SERP) is a set of benefits that may be made available to top-level employees in addition to those covered in the company's standard retirement savings plan.</p><p>A SERP is a form of a deferred-compensation plan. It is not a qualified plan. That is, there is no special tax treatment for the company or the employee, such as is available through a 401(k) plan.</p>
