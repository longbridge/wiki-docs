# High Street Bank

<ArticleMeta id={100465} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:32:23'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>High Street Banks, also known as Retail Banks, are banks that primarily serve individual consumers and small businesses by offering a wide range of financial services. These banks are typically located on the main streets of cities and towns, hence the name "High Street Banks." Services provided by high street banks include savings accounts, checking accounts, loans, mortgages, credit cards, insurance, and investment products.</p><p>The key characteristic of high street banks is their focus on the general public, emphasizing customer service and convenience. They usually have extensive branch networks and numerous ATMs to facilitate everyday banking transactions for their customers.</p>
