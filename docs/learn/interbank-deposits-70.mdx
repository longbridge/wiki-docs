# Interbank Deposits

<ArticleMeta id={70} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:07:45'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Interbank deposits refer to funds that banks deposit with each other. Typically, banks will place their excess funds with other banks to earn interest or to meet liquidity needs. Interbank deposits are an important tool for short-term fund management among banks.</p>
