# Asset disposal income

<ArticleMeta id={260} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:58:20'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Asset disposal income refers to the income obtained by a company through the sale or disposal of assets. This includes income from the sale of assets, the recovery of debt, compensation payments, and so on. Asset disposal income is usually included in the company's income statement and has an impact on its operating performance.
