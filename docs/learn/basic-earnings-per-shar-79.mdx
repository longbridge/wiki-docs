# Basic Earnings Per Shar

<ArticleMeta id={79} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:07:45'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Earnings per share (EPS) refers to the amount of net profit generated by a company in a certain accounting period, distributed on a per share basis to common shareholders. It is one of the important indicators for investors to evaluate the profitability of stocks.

<AIContent content={`<p><strong>Definition:</strong> Basic Earnings Per Share (EPS) refers to the amount of net profit a company generates in a given accounting period, distributed on a per-share basis to common shareholders.
