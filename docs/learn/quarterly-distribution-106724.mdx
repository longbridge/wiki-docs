# Quarterly Distribution

<ArticleMeta id={106724} updatedAt={'2023-12-02 08:20:05'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Quarterly dividend refers to the act of distributing profits to shareholders in the form of cash or stock on a quarterly basis. Quarterly dividend is a common way of dividend distribution, aiming to reward shareholders and increase their return on investment.

The advantage of this method is that shareholders can get immediate returns, but the downside is that it may affect the company's cash flow.</li><li><strong>Stock Dividends:</strong> The company distributes part of its profits to shareholders in the form of additional shares.
