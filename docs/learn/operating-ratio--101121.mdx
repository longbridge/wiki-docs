# Operating Ratio 

<ArticleMeta id={101121} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Operating Ratio is an important financial metric that measures the efficiency of a company's operations. It represents the proportion of operating costs to total revenue, typically expressed as a percentage. A lower operating ratio indicates that the company is more efficient in managing its operating costs, while a higher ratio suggests potential issues in cost control.</p>
