Improvements may be undertaken by the landlord or the tenant and may be paid by the tenant.While the useful economic life of most leasehold improvements is anywhere between five and 10 years, the Internal Revenue Code (IRC) requires that depreciation for such improvements to occur over the economic life of the building.
