# Shareholders' Equity

<ArticleMeta id={251} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:59:39'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Shareholders' Equity represents the residual interest in the assets of a company after deducting liabilities, indicating the ownership interest of shareholders in the company's net assets. Shareholders' equity includes components such as share capital, additional paid-in capital, and retained earnings. It reflects the company's ability to create value for its shareholders and is a crucial indicator of the company's financial health. Increases in shareholders' equity typically result from increased profitability and additional investments from shareholders.</p>
