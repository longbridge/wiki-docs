# Counterparty Risk

<ArticleMeta id={101979} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:58'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>Counterparty risk refers to the risk in financial transactions that one party may fail to fulfill its contractual obligations. This type of risk is prevalent in credit, investment, and various trading activities and significantly impacts the decision-making of financial institutions and investors.</span></p>

Early financial transactions relied heavily on trust and reputation, but as the scale and complexity of transactions increased, managing counterparty risk became more critical.
