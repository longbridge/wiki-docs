# Bond Valuation

<ArticleMeta id={101161} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Bond Valuation is the process of determining the intrinsic value of a bond by calculating its present value. The purpose of bond valuation is to assess the fair price of a bond under current market conditions. The present value of a bond is the discounted value of its future cash flows, which include periodic interest payments and the principal repayment at maturity. The discount rate is usually based on the market interest rate or the investor's required rate of return.&nbsp;</p>
