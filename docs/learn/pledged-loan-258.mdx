# Pledged Loan

<ArticleMeta id={258} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:58:22'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Pledged loan refers to the act of a borrower using a valuable property or asset owned by themselves as collateral for a loan and providing collateral to the creditor in order to obtain the loan.

<AIContent content={`<h2>Pledge Loan</h2><h3>Definition</h3><p>A pledge loan refers to the act of a borrower using valuable property or assets they own as collateral to secure a loan from a creditor.
