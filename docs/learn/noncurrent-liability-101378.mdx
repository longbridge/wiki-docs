# Noncurrent Liability

<ArticleMeta id={101378} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Noncurrent Liabilities refer to debts or obligations that a company does not need to settle within one accounting year (or one normal operating cycle). These liabilities typically have a longer repayment period and reflect the company's long-term debt and financing situation.</p>
