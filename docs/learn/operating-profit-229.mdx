# Operating Profit

<ArticleMeta id={229} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:00:36'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Operating profit refers to the profit obtained by a company in its operating activities and is one of the important indicators in the income statement. It is the profit obtained by deducting operating costs such as cost of sales, administrative expenses, and financial expenses from the company's revenue.
