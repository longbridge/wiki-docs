# Symmetrical Distribution

<ArticleMeta id={100395} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A symmetrical distribution refers to a data distribution where the shape is mirrored around its central axis, meaning the left and right sides of the distribution are mirror images of each other. In a symmetrical distribution, the mean, median, and mode of the data are typically equal or very close to each other.</p>
