# Barbell Strategy

<ArticleMeta id={100463} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Barbell Strategy is an investment approach that aims to balance risk and return by holding both high-risk and low-risk assets in a portfolio simultaneously. The strategy gets its name from its visual analogy: the portfolio is weighted at both ends with high-risk and low-risk assets, while the middle part (medium-risk assets) is less represented or absent.</p>
