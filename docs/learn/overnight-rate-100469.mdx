# Overnight Rate

<ArticleMeta id={100469} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The overnight rate is the interest rate at which financial institutions lend or borrow funds from one another in the overnight market. This type of borrowing typically occurs to meet short-term liquidity needs, such as a bank borrowing funds to meet its reserve requirements with the central bank. The overnight rate is the shortest-term interest rate in the money market and significantly influences overall market liquidity and interest rate levels.</p>
