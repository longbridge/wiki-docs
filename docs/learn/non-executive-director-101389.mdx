# Non-Executive Director

<ArticleMeta id={101389} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Non-Executive Director (NED) is a member of a company's board of directors who does not engage in the day-to-day management of the organization. Their primary role is to provide independent oversight and constructive criticism during board meetings and committee activities, ensuring transparency and fairness in the company's governance and decision-making processes. Non-Executive Directors typically do not hold other management positions within the company, which allows them to offer an objective perspective, helping to balance the interests of stakeholders and monitor the actions of the executive management.</p>
