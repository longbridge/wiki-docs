# Common Stock Equity

<ArticleMeta id={159} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:03:45'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Common stock capital refers to the total amount of common stock issued by a company. Common stock represents the equity portion that shareholders hold in the company, and shareholders can enjoy the rights to dividends and other distributions declared by the company. Common stock capital reflects the equity of the company's shareholders. Common stock capital can be calculated by multiplying the per share market price of common stock by the total number of common stock issued.
