# Net Accounts Receivable

<ArticleMeta id={124} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:05:28'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Net accounts receivable refers to the balance of accounts receivable after deducting the allowance for bad debts, which represents the actual amount of accounts receivable that the company can collect.
