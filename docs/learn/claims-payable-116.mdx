# Claims payable

<ArticleMeta id={116} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:05:52'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Claims payable refers to the compensation amount that an insurance company should pay to the policyholder or insured when an insurance accident occurs and meets the claim conditions according to the insurance contract. The insurance company fulfills its contractual obligations by paying the compensation to the insured party.</p>
