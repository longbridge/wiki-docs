# Distribution Management

<ArticleMeta id={100432} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Distribution management refers to the process of planning, implementing, and controlling the distribution channels and networks to effectively deliver products or services to the final consumers or users. The main objective of distribution management is to ensure that products or services reach the target market at the right time and place, in the most economical way, thereby meeting customer demands and maximizing company profits.</p>
