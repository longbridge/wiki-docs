# Gross Net Written Premium Income

<ArticleMeta id={102045} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:59:00'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(6,6,7)"}}>Gross Net Written Premium Income (GNWPI) is the amount of an insurance company's premiums that are actually recorded as income after deductions for cancellations and refunds. It serves as the basis for calculating the portion of premiums to be paid to reinsurers, considering also the premiums paid for reinsurance coverage. GNWPI is crucial for the financial health and reinsurance strategy of an insurance company.</span></p>
