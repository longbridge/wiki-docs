# Tracking Error

<ArticleMeta id={100473} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:31:52'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Tracking error is a statistical measure that quantifies the difference in performance between an investment portfolio (such as an index fund or ETF) and its benchmark index. Specifically, tracking error represents the standard deviation of the difference between the portfolio's returns and the benchmark index's returns. A lower tracking error indicates that the portfolio closely replicates the performance of the benchmark index, while a higher tracking error suggests a greater deviation from the benchmark. Tracking error helps investors assess the effectiveness of a fund manager's investment strategy and management skills.</p>
