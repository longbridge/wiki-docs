# class action lawsuits

<ArticleMeta id={103504} updatedAt={'2023-10-07 02:24:55'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Class action refers to a group of people who unite to sue an institution or company to protect their common interests. Class actions are usually led by a representative or representative lawyer who negotiates or litigates on behalf of all affected individuals.
