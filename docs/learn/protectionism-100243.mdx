# Protectionism

<ArticleMeta id={100243} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Protectionism is an economic policy approach where a country implements various measures to shield its domestic industries from foreign competition. These measures can include tariffs, quotas, subsidies, import restrictions, and other non-tariff barriers. The primary objectives of protectionism are to protect domestic jobs, prevent unemployment, safeguard national security, and promote economic self-sufficiency. However, protectionism can also lead to strained relations between trading partners, reduce economic efficiency, increase costs for consumers, and potentially trigger trade wars.</p>
