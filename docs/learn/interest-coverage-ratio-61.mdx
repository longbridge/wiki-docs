# Interest coverage ratio

<ArticleMeta id={61} updatedAt={'2024-12-05 18:07:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Interest coverage ratio refers to the multiple of operating cash flow available for interest payment before paying interest. It is used to measure a company's debt-paying ability and debt risk. A higher interest coverage ratio indicates a stronger debt-paying ability for the company.
