# Kyoto Protocol

<ArticleMeta id={101461} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 10:41:15'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(17,17,17)"}}>The Kyoto Protocol was an international agreement that aimed to reduce carbon dioxide emissions and the presence of greenhouse gases (GHG) in the atmosphere. The essential tenet of the Kyoto Protocol was that industrialized nations needed to reduce their CO2 emissions. The protocol was adopted in Kyoto, Japan, in 1997, as greenhouse gas emissions threatened climate stability. It was effectively replaced by the Paris Agreement, which went into effect in 2016.</span></p>
