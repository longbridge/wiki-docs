# Research and Development Expense Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={199} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 21:01:50'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The Research and Development Expense Ratio (R&amp;D Expense Ratio) is the percentage of a company's total revenue that is spent on research and development over a specific period. This metric is used to gauge the extent of a company's investment in R&amp;D activities and its strategic focus on innovation and technology development. A higher R&amp;D expense ratio typically indicates a strong emphasis on technological innovation and product development, but it may also impact short-term profitability.</p>
