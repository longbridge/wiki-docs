# Cook The Books

<ArticleMeta id={100288} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>"Cook The Books" refers to the act of deliberately falsifying financial statements by a company or individual to mislead investors, regulators, or other stakeholders. This behavior typically involves inflating revenues, hiding debts, manipulating costs or expenses, among other tactics, to make the company's financial situation appear better than it actually is. Cooking the books is a serious form of financial fraud and can lead to legal action, fines, damage to the company's reputation, and even bankruptcy.</p>
