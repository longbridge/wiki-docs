# Profitability Factor

<ArticleMeta id={324} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:55:09'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Profitability factor refers to financial metrics and data used to assess a company's profitability. Common profitability factors include net profit margin, gross profit margin, return on assets (ROA), and return on equity (ROE). Investors analyze these profitability factors to evaluate a company's earning ability and financial health, making investment decisions accordingly. Profitability factors are important in both value-oriented and growth-oriented investment strategies.</p>
