# Growth and Income Fund

<ArticleMeta id={102288} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:59:00'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Growth and Income Fund is a type of hybrid fund designed to achieve the dual objectives of capital appreciation and stable income by investing in securities with both growth potential and income generation. These funds typically invest in growth stocks, income stocks, and other assets that can provide dividends or interest. The goal of a Growth and Income Fund is to offer both long-term capital appreciation and current income distribution, thereby balancing risk and return. This type of fund is an ideal choice for investors who seek to enjoy capital growth while also receiving regular income.</p>
