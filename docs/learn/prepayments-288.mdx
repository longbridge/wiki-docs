# Prepayments

<ArticleMeta id={288} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:56:34'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Prepaid expenses, also known as prepaid accounts, refer to the amounts a company pays in advance to suppliers or other business partners for products or services. Prepaid expenses are generally listed as assets on the balance sheet until the company receives the corresponding goods or services.</p>
