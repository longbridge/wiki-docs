# Marginal Contribution

<ArticleMeta id={100487} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Marginal Contribution refers to the additional profit generated by producing one more unit of a product or service. It is typically used to measure the contribution of each unit of product or service to the overall profit, calculated as sales revenue minus variable costs. Marginal contribution is an important financial metric that helps businesses understand the profitability of their products or services.</p>
