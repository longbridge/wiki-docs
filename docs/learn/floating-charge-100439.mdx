# Floating Charge

<ArticleMeta id={100439} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A floating charge is a form of security interest typically used in corporate finance. It allows the borrower to continue to use and manage their assets without hindrance. The floating charge covers a class or all of the company's assets without attaching to specific items. It "crystallizes" into a fixed charge upon certain trigger events, such as the company's insolvency or default, thereby attaching to specific assets.</p>
