# Income Statement

<ArticleMeta id={100171} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>An income statement is one of the three important financial statements used for reporting a company’s&nbsp;financial performance&nbsp;over a specific accounting period. The other two key statements are the balance sheet&nbsp;and the cash flow statement.</p><p>The income statement focuses on the revenue,&nbsp;expenses, gains, and losses reported by a company during a particular period. Also known as the profit and loss (P&amp;L) statement or the statement of revenue and expense, an income statement provides valuable insights into a company’s operations, the efficiency of its management, underperforming sectors, and its performance relative to industry peers.</p>
