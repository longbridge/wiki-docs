# Pullback

<p><span style={{backgroundColor:"rgb(255,255,255)",color:"rgb(17,17,17)"}}>A pullback is a pause or moderate drop in a stock or commodities pricing chart from recent peaks that occur within a continuing uptrend. A pullback is very similar to retracement or consolidation, and the terms are sometimes used interchangeably. The term pullback is usually applied to pricing drops that are relatively short in duration—for example, a few consecutive sessions—before the uptrend resumes.</span></p>
