# capital gains

<ArticleMeta id={103067} updatedAt={'2023-09-29 08:20:55'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Capital gains refer to profits obtained through the sale of assets. Capital gains can come from the appreciation or sale of investments such as stocks, bonds, real estate, etc. Capital gains are a way for investors to obtain investment returns.
