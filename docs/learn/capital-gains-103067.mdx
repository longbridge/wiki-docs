# Capital Gain

<ArticleMeta id={103067} updatedAt={'2023-09-28 17:20:55'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Capital gain refers to the increase in value of an asset (such as stocks or real estate) when it is sold for more than its purchase price. Capital gains are a way for investors to realize profits and are typically subject to capital gains tax.</p>
