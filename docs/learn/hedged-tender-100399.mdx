# Hedged Tender

<ArticleMeta id={100399} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A Hedged Tender is a financial strategy commonly used in mergers, acquisitions, or corporate restructuring. It combines a tender offer with hedging strategies to mitigate risks associated with market volatility and price fluctuations. In a hedged tender, the bidder not only makes an offer to purchase shares of the target company but also employs hedging instruments, such as options or futures, to manage and control the market risks related to the tender offer.</p>
