# Cash Inflows from Financing Activities

<ArticleMeta id={244} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:59:53'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Cash inflows from financing activities refer to the amount of cash generated from financing activities. Financing activities refer to the activities of raising funds through issuing stocks or debts, etc. Cash inflows from financing activities mainly include cash from the issuance of stocks, cash from borrowing, cash from investment absorption, etc.
