# Vertical Equity

<ArticleMeta id={100327} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Vertical Equity is a principle of taxation that advocates for taxing individuals based on their ability to pay. This means that those with higher incomes should pay more in taxes, while those with lower incomes should pay less. The core idea of vertical equity is that the tax system should be progressive, ensuring a fair distribution of the tax burden across different income levels to promote social equity. Vertical equity is commonly associated with a progressive tax system, where tax rates increase as income increases.</p>
