# Growth Curve

<ArticleMeta id={101158} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Growth Curve is a graphical representation used to show the growth trend of a variable over time. In fields such as economics, finance, biology, and others, growth curves are often used to analyze and predict growth patterns. For example, in corporate financial analysis, a growth curve can illustrate the changes in a company's revenue, profit, or user numbers over time.</p>
