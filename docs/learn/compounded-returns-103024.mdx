# Compound Annual Growth Rate

<ArticleMeta id={103024} updatedAt={'2024-09-11 23:18:44'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Compound annual growth rate (CAGR) refers to the average annualized return of an investment portfolio or asset over a period of time. Unlike simple returns, CAGR takes into account the compounding effect of investment returns, providing a more accurate reflection of the true performance of the asset. CAGR takes into account the effect of compounding, providing a more accurate measure of an investment's growth over time by smoothing out annual fluctuations.</p>
