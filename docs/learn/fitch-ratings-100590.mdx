# Fitch Ratings

<ArticleMeta id={100590} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:24:52'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Fitch Ratings is a globally recognized credit rating agency, founded in 1914, with headquarters in New York and London. Fitch Ratings primarily provides credit ratings for countries, corporations, financial institutions, and securities, helping investors understand the credit risk associated with these entities. Fitch's rating scale ranges from the highest rating of AAA to the lowest rating of D, reflecting the credit quality and default risk of the rated entity. As one of the three major international rating agencies (alongside Standard &amp; Poor's and Moody's), Fitch Ratings' assessments hold significant influence in the global financial markets and are widely used for investment decisions and risk management.</p>
