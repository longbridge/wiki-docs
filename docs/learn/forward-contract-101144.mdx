# Forward Contract

<ArticleMeta id={101144} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A forward contract is a financial instrument that represents an agreement between two parties to buy or sell an asset at a predetermined price on a specific future date. Unlike futures contracts, forward contracts are typically traded over-the-counter (OTC) and can be customized according to the needs of the parties involved. Forward contracts are primarily used for hedging price fluctuation risks or for speculation.</p>
