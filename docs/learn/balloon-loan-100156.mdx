# Balloon Loan

<ArticleMeta id={100156} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:47:57'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A balloon loan is a type of loan that does not fully amortize over its term. Since it is not fully amortized, a balloon payment is required at the end of the term to repay the remaining balance of the loan.</p><p>Balloon loans can be attractive to short-term borrowers because they typically carry lower interest rates than loans with longer terms. However, the borrower must be aware of refinancing risks&nbsp;as there's a possibility the loan may reset at a higher interest rate.</p>
