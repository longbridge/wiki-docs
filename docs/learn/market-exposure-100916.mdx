# Market Exposure

<ArticleMeta id={100916} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:51'} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Market exposure refers to the dollar amount of funds or percentage of a broader portfolio that is invested in a particular type of security, market sector, or industry. Market exposure is usually expressed as a percentage of total portfolio holdings, for instance, as in 10% of a portfolio being exposed to the oil and gas sector or a $ 50,000 in Tesla stock.Market exposure represents the amount an investor can lose from the risks unique to a particular investment or asset class.
