# Bond Ladder

<ArticleMeta id={101162} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Bond Ladder is an investment strategy that involves purchasing multiple bonds with different maturity dates to diversify risk and manage cash flow. In this strategy, an investor buys a series of bonds with varying maturities, creating a portfolio that resembles a ladder. &nbsp;When the shortest-term bond matures, the investor can reinvest the principal in a new long-term bond, thereby maintaining the ladder structure. This strategy helps to reduce interest rate risk because the bonds in the portfolio mature and are reinvested over time.&nbsp;</p>
