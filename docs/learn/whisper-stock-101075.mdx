# Whisper Stock

<ArticleMeta id={101075} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:00:12'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>A public company's shares can briefly become a whisper stock if rumors circulate that the company is the target of a takeover offer. An insider who acts on the information in an attempt to make a profit or help someone else make a profit is committing the crime of insider trading.</p>
