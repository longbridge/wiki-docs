# Hook Reversal

<p>A Hook Reversal is a price pattern in technical analysis that typically appears at the top or bottom of a market trend, indicating a potential reversal of the current trend. The hook reversal pattern consists of two parts: first, a clear trend (upward or downward), followed by a sudden price reversal that forms a hook-like shape.</p>
