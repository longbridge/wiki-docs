# Liquidity Factor

<ArticleMeta id={319} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:55:27'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The liquidity factor refers to metrics used to assess an asset's ability to be quickly converted into cash in the market. Common liquidity factors include trading volume and bid-ask spread. Highly liquid assets are easier to buy and sell with lower transaction costs. The liquidity factor is crucial for portfolio management and risk control.</p>
