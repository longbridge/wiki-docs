# Flat Tax

<ArticleMeta id={100973} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:51'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

A flat tax refers to a single tax rate being applied to every taxpayer regardless of income. Typically, a flat tax involves no deductions or exemptions. However, some politicians have proposed flat tax systems that keep certain deductions in place.Most flat tax systems or proposals do not tax income from dividends, distributions, capital gains, or other investments.
