# Financial expenses

<ArticleMeta id={254} updatedAt={'2024-09-12 20:59:24'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Financial expenses refer to the interest expenses and other expenses incurred by a company in the process of operating due to the use of borrowed funds. Financial expenses include interest expenses, handling fees, exchange gains and losses, etc. Financial expenses are part of a company's operating activities and reflect the company's financing costs.
