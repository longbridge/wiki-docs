# Fringe Benefits

<ArticleMeta id={100442} updatedAt={'2023-09-25 22:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Fringe benefits, also known as perquisites (perks) or additional benefits, refer to extra compensation or benefits provided by employers to employees beyond their regular wages or salaries. These benefits can include company cars, health insurance, housing allowances, educational subsidies, paid vacations, employee discounts, gym memberships, meal allowances, and more. The purpose of fringe benefits is to increase the overall compensation for employees, enhance job satisfaction and loyalty, and help companies attract and retain top talent.</p>
