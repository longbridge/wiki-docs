# Dividend Coverage Ratio

<ArticleMeta id={222} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:00:54'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Dividend coverage ratio refers to the proportion of the current year's distributable profit to the share capital held by shareholders. A higher dividend coverage ratio indicates a stronger ability of the company to pay dividends and a higher level of dividend protection for shareholders.
