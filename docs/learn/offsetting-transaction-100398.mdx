# Offsetting Transaction

<ArticleMeta id={100398} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>An offsetting transaction is a financial operation where an investor takes a position opposite to an original transaction to reduce or eliminate the risk or exposure of the initial trade. For example, if an investor holds a long position in an asset (i.e., has bought it), they can offset this position by selling the same quantity of the asset, thereby neutralizing the risk from market fluctuations.</p>
