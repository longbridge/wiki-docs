# Income Tax Expense

<ArticleMeta id={139} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 12:04:24'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

Income tax expense refers to the income tax that a company needs to pay during its business activities. It is a part of the company's profit, and when calculating the net profit of the company, the profit needs to be reduced by the income tax expense. Income tax expense is an important component of the company's tax cost.
