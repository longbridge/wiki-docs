# Rising Interest Rates

<ArticleMeta id={102840} updatedAt={'2023-09-27 05:22:10'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Rising interest rates refer to an increase in the benchmark interest rate set by central banks or other financial institutions, leading to higher borrowing costs in the market. Rising interest rates are typically used to curb inflation and prevent an overheating economy but may also dampen consumer spending, investment, and economic activity.</p><p>Rising interest rates will have a negative impact on borrowers, as borrowing costs will increase, while savers will have a positive impact as they can earn higher savings rates.</p>
