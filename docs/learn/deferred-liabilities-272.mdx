# Deferred Liabilities

<ArticleMeta id={272} updatedAt={'2024-09-13 11:57:49'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>Deferred liabilities are obligations that a company has incurred during the current accounting period but will not pay until a future date. These liabilities typically include deferred taxes, deferred revenue, pension obligations, and lease liabilities. Deferred liabilities are listed as long-term liabilities on the company's balance sheet, indicating the company's future payment obligations.</p>
