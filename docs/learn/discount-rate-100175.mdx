# Discount Rate

<ArticleMeta id={100175} updatedAt={'2023-09-26 13:58:47'} alias={`[]`} />
<div className='border-solid border-b border-t-0 my-4 border-[var(--ifm-color-gray-300)]' />

<p>The discount rate is the interest rate the Federal Reserve charges commercial banks&nbsp;and other financial institutions for short-term loans. The discount rate is applied at the Fed's lending facility, which is called the discount window.</p><p>A discount rate can also refer to the interest rate used in discounted cash flow (DCF) analysis to determine the present value&nbsp;of future cash flows.&nbsp;In this case, investors and businesses can use the discount rate for potential investments.</p>
