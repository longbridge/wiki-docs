# Marketing Strategy

<ArticleMeta viewCount={102106} updatedAt={'1700-12-19 16:00:00'} />

<p>A marketing strategy refers to a business’s overall game plan for reaching prospective consumers and turning them into customers of their products or services. A marketing strategy contains the company’s value proposition, key brand messaging, data on target customer demographics, and other high-level elements.A thorough marketing strategy covers the four Ps of marketing: product, price, place, and promotion.</p>
