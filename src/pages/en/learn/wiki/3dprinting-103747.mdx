# 3D printing

<ArticleMeta viewCount={103747} updatedAt={'1697-06-24 14:00:00'} />

3D printing is a technology that creates objects through digital models. It uses various materials such as plastic, metal, and concrete to print three-dimensional objects layer by layer. 3D printing technology has been widely used in fields such as healthcare, manufacturing, and construction.
