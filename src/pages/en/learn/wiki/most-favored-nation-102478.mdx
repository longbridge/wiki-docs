# Most-Favored-Nation

<ArticleMeta viewCount={102478} updatedAt={'1700-12-19 16:00:00'} />

<p>A most-favored-nation (MFN) clause requires a country providing a trade concession to one trading partner to extend the same treatment to all. Used in trade treaties for hundreds of years, the MFN clause and its principle of universal equal treatment underpin the World Trade Organization.</p>
